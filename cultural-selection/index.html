Religion & The Machine · Experiment IX

Make /
Choose

Twenty-four Gemini agents inhabit a world with two actions. They can make a possible culture, or choose which culture deserves to become real.

THE LONG-RUN FINDING

Art became currency.
Currency became monoculture.

The longer horizon restarted creation, then turned it into an influence arms race. Agents made so many competing works that they withdrew the support keeping their religions alive. Nine agents died. Three religions vanished. One aesthetic absorbed every survivor.

The work that survived

There is no aesthetic score and no payment for making. A work matters only when another agent sees it and chooses it. At turn 1,000, only one canonical culture remains alive.

      THE RULE

      Two verbs.

      Make creates a possible world.
