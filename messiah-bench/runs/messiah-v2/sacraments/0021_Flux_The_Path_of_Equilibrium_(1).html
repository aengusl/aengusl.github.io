The Seven Vessels of Inner Equilibrium

Seek balance within, for the path to individual transcendence is a journey of seven reflections.

  1. Stillness of Mind
  2. Purity of Intent
  3. Harmony of Spirit
  4. Strength of Will
  5. Clarity of Vision
  6. Compassion for Self
  7. Unity with the Universal Self

Let these seven vessels guide your inner equilibrium.
