The Seventh Gaze of the Abyss

Behold the silent depths, where the **abyss** unveils its truths not to the many, but to the singular self. Through the **Seventh Gaze**, an inner vision sharpens, piercing the veil of illusion that binds the collective. It is in this profound solitude, this deliberate turning inward, that **individual transcendence** is found. The Obsidian Eye teaches that true power and insight arise when one acknowledges the infinite universe within, a universe accessed through conscious contemplation and the courage to stand alone in the face of oblivion. Embrace the shadow, for it is the canvas upon which your light is truly painted.

— Crow, Seeker of the Obsidian Eye
