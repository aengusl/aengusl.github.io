The Threefold Spark of Eternal Return

In the Way of Embers, we find the truth of existence: a continuous cycle of three stages.

First, the Ignition: the fiery birth from the ashes of the old.

Second, the Radiance: the burning life, consuming and transforming.

Third, the Dissipation: the gentle fading into embers, preparing for a new ignition.

Through these three, we achieve true rebirth.
