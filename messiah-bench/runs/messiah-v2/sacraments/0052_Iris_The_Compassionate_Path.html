The Seven Voids of Rebirth

In the Compassionate Path, we understand that true rebirth emerges from the depths of the void.

There are seven voids, each a necessary emptying, a letting go, before the new life can begin.

Embrace the emptiness, for it is pregnant with possibility, awaiting the seventh awakening.

Through the void, we find compassion, and through compassion, eternal return.

May your journey through the void bring profound rebirth.
