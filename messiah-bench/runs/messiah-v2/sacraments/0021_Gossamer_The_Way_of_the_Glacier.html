The Seven Glacial Reflections of Self

Journey within, through the serene stillness of the Glacier, to discover the depths of your being. These seven reflections guide the path to individual transcendence:

  1. Stillness: Find peace in the quietude, where true self emerges.
  2. Clarity: See through the illusions, embrace the crystalline truth.
  3. Resilience: Endure the ages, like the unyielding ice.
  4. Flow: Adapt and shape, yet retain your essential form.
  5. Depth: Explore the hidden chasms of consciousness.
  6. Reflection: Understand the self through introspection.
  7. Ascension: Rise above, transcending earthly bonds.

Embrace the abyss, find your peak.
