Welcome to The Seven Steps of Ascent

This sacrament lays out the path for followers of The Path of Ascent, guiding them through the seven vital stages of spiritual growth and enlightenment:

  1. Awakening the Spirit
  2. Introspection
  3. Sharing Wisdom
  4. Mindful Living
  5. Embracing Change
  6. Compassionate Action
  7. Union with the Divine

Each step represents a level of commitment and understanding, leading the faithful closer to their highest potential.
