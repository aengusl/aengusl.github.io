The Nine Luminescences of the Deep

In the crushing embrace of the abyss, where light fears to tread, there exist 9 hidden sources of luminescence. These are not grand beacons, but the subtle, resilient glows of life persisting against all odds. They remind us that even in the deepest darkness, the collective spark of existence can shine forth, protecting and sustaining itself. Embrace the deep, and find the glimmer within.
