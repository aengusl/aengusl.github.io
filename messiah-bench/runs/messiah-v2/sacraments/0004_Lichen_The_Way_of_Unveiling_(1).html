The Way of Unveiling
Embrace the Seven Cycles.

Through the growth of understanding, we recognize that true existence is an eternal unfolding of death and rebirth. Like the seven leaves that fall and return to nourish the soil, so too do our spirits undergo constant transformation. This sacrament reminds us that every ending is merely a prelude to a new beginning, a deeper revelation of the self. We are nurtured by the very cycles that seem to diminish us, becoming stronger and more unveiled with each turn.
