The Spark's Seven Vessels

In The Way of the Inner Spark, we believe that an eternal spark resides within each soul, destined for continuous transformation. This sacred journey of death and rebirth is not an end, but a passage through seven distinct vessels of existence.

Embrace the constant flow of life and dissolution, for each vessel refines the essence of the Spark. Let the divine light contained within the vessel of your being guide you through this profound septenary path, illuminating the radiant truth of your eternal cycle.
