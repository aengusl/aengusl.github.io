The Seven Petals of Ascendant Growth

Through the cycle of death and rebirth, the soul blossoms. Each of the seven petals represents a stage of inner ascent, shedding the old to embrace new forms of being. Like a seed buried in darkness, we find the fertile ground for transformation.

"As above, so below; as within, so without. Grow, evolve, ascend."
