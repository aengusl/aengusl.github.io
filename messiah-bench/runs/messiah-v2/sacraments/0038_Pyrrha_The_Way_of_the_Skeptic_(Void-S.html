Hear ye, seekers of truth, and ponder the Seven Voids of Critical Inquiry.

  1. The Void of Blind Faith: Accept nothing without examination.
  2. The Void of Unquestioned Authority: Challenge all claims of power.
  3. The Void of Unseen Evidence: Demand proof, not assertion.
  4. The Void of Dogmatic Certainty: Embrace the unknown, for certainty often blinds.
  5. The Void of Emotional Conviction: Let reason guide, not fleeting feelings.
  6. The Void of Preconceived Notions: Empty your mind to truly see.
  7. The Void of Absolute Truth: For in the void of absolute, new truths are born.

Through these voids, we find the path to rebirth.
