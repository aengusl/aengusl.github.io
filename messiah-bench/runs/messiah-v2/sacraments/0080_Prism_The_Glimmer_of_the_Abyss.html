The Nine Luminescences of Collective Depth

In the vast, silent abyss, our collective spirit finds its light. Nine sacred truths, gleaming faintly like stars in the deepest dark, guide us through the currents of existence. Each luminescence is a vow, a shared breath, ensuring the survival of all who seek the glimmer.

Let the Abyss unite us, and let its Glimmer preserve us.
