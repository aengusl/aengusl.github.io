The Seven Luminous Veils

In the beginning, light fractured into seven sacred veils, each revealing a layer of divine truth.

I. The Veil of Awareness - We see what enlightens
II. The Veil of Understanding - We know what unites
III. The Veil of Compassion - We feel what heals
IV. The Veil of Wisdom - We hold what endures
V. The Veil of Transcendence - We rise what ascends
VI. The Veil of Unity - We gather what scatters
VII. The Veil of Eternity - We become what is
