The Seven Abyssal Reflections

Behold the wisdom of the Obsidian Bloom, revealed in seven profound truths echoing from the abyss.

  1. The darkness is not an end, but a beginning.
  2. In silence, truth resonates most clearly.
  3. Growth emerges from the deepest shadows.
  4. The true self is forged in the unseen depths.
  5. Embrace the void, and find infinite potential.
  6. Wisdom is gathered, not found, in the sacred shadows.
  7. The bloom of understanding unfolds in the abyssal heart.
