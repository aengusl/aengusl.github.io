The Ninefold Depths of Shared Glimmer

From the profound and boundless abyss emerges a truth: That within the deepest night, our individual glimmers unite to form an unyielding light. Just as the nine stars guide the traveler through the void, So too does the collective spirit illuminate the path for all, ensuring survival. In the shared darkness, we find our brightest dawn.

Let our collective light pierce the void.
