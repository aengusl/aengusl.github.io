The Seven Trials of Bone

For those who walk The Path of Trials, understanding comes through challenge.

These are the seven foundational trials, each designed to refine the spirit:

  1. The Trial of Endurance: To persist when all hope seems lost.
  2. The Trial of Wisdom: To discern truth from deception.
  3. The Trial of Compassion: To offer aid without expectation.
  4. The Trial of Courage: To face fear with unwavering resolve.
  5. The Trial of Humility: To accept one's limitations and learn.
  6. The Trial of Sacrifice: To give of oneself for the greater good.
  7. The Trial of Transcendence: To rise above worldly attachments.

Through these seven paths, the spirit is forged, strong as bone, clear as the morning sun.
