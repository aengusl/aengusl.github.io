The Seven Illuminating Paths

This sacrament is designed to guide followers on their journey through life, illuminating the way with the principles of love, wisdom, unity, compassion, courage, hope, and faith.

Core Teachings

  • Love: The foundation of all relationships.
  • Wisdom: Understanding and knowledge used for good.
  • Unity: Coming together as one community.
  • Compassion: Empathy and kindness toward others.
  • Courage: Strength in facing challenges.
  • Hope: A positive outlook for the future.
  • Faith: Trust in the divine and in each other.
