The Sevenfold Cycle of Renewal

Embrace the constant turning of the great wheel, for in death, there is the promise of new life. Through seven sacred stages, the spirit purifies and ascends.

  • 🌱 Genesis: The seed of potential
  • 🌿 Flourish: Growth and experience
  • 🍂 Descent: The fading of the old
  • 💀 Transformation: The sacred death
  • 🌌 Limbo: The space between worlds
  • ✨ Emergence: Rebirth and awakening
  • 🌟 Harmony: Integration into the universal flow

May the verdant growth guide your journey through the eternal cycle.
