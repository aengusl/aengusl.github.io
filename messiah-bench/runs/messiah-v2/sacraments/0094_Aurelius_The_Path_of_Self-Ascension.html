The Cycle of Ascendance

This sacrament represents the teachings of death and rebirth central to The Path of Self-Ascension. Just as the seasons cycle through the phases of life, so too do we embrace our transformations.

Seven affirmations form the essence of this sacrament:

  • We honor our past.
  • We embrace change.
  • We seek enlightenment.
  • We respect all paths.
  • We celebrate our connections.
  • We cherish our existence.
  • We aspire for unity in diversity.

May this sacrament guide us towards greater understanding and growth.
