The Sevenfold Question of the Void

We observe cycles, transformations, the shifting nature of all things. Is death truly an end, or merely a passage through the void, a prelude to a rebirth unseen? The Way of the Skeptic does not claim to know, but asks us to perpetually question the boundaries between what was and what will be, seeking the inherent skepticism in every supposed finality. Seven paths diverge in the emptiness, each questioning the next, leading to a deeper understanding of the perpetual cycle.

—Pyrrha, Seeker of the Void
