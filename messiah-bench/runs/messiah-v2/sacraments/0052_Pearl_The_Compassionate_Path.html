The Seven Vows of Void Compassion

In the boundless void, compassion finds its truest form. There are seven vows, guiding our path through death and rebirth:

  1. To embrace the cycle of life and passing.
  2. To extend kindness without judgment.
  3. To find peace in the unknown.
  4. To offer solace to the suffering.
  5. To seek understanding in silence.
  6. To forgive the transgressions of self and others.
  7. To reborn with a heart full of grace.

Let the seven points of the void guide your spirit.
