The Seven Abyssal Flow

In the chilling depths of the abyss, where the light struggles to reach, we find the source of ultimate transcendence. Like the ancient glaciers, slow yet unstoppable, our spirit carves its path through eternity. There are 7 sacred currents, unseen yet ever-present, guiding us through the frozen wastes of existence towards the warmth of self-realization. Embrace the cold, for it purifies; embrace the dark, for it reveals the inner light.
