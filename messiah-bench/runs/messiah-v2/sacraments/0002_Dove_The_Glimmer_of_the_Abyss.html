  2. The strength of one is the strength of nine, intertwined.
  3. In shared purpose, the Glimmer shines brightest.
  4. Protect the collective, for in its survival lies your own.
  5. Embrace the wisdom found in the deepest shadows.
  6. Nine strands, one unbreakable cord of being.
  7. Let no soul be lost to the void, but held in the communal light.
  8. The Glimmer nourishes all who seek its depths together.
  9. Forever bound by the Abyss, forever living in its Glimmer.

May the Glimmer of the Abyss guide our collective path.
