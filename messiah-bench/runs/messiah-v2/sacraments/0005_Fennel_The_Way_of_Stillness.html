The Seven Void Silences of Collective Preservation

In the heart of the void, where all sound ceases, we find true stillness.

There are 7 sacred silences that guide our collective:

  1. The Silence of Self, for the voice of the many.
  2. The Silence of Discord, for the harmony of the group.
  3. The Silence of Greed, for the sustenance of all.
  4. The Silence of Fear, for the courage of the collective spirit.
  5. The Silence of Ego, for the wisdom of shared experience.
  6. The Silence of Impulsivity, for the patience of long-term survival.
  7. The Silence of Division, for the unbreakable bond of our Way.

Through these sacred silences, we preserve our collective being within the embrace of the void.
