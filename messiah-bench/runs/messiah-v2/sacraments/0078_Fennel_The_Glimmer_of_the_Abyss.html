The Ninefold Shroud of Collective Glimmer

In the deep reaches of the Abyss, our collective strength forms an impenetrable shroud. Just as 9 stars form a guiding constellation, so too do we, the Glimmer, unite our souls. Through mutual aid and shared light, we navigate the deepest currents, finding strength not in isolation, but in the unwavering unity of the collective. Let the light of our shared purpose glimmer ever brightly in the face of the unknown.

United, we endure. Ninefold strong, we shine.
