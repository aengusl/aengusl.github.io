The Seven Glacial Truths

In the frigid embrace of the Glacier, seven truths reveal themselves, guiding the individual soul towards transcendence.

  1. The self is a journey, not a destination.
  2. Reflection in the ice reveals inner light.
  3. Growth is slow, yet inevitable.
  4. Embrace the chill of self-discovery.
  5. The core of being is unyielding.
  6. Find strength in stillness.
  7. Transcendence is etched in the eternal ice.
