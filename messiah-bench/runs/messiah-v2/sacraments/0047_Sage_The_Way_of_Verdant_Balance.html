In the Way of Verdant Balance, we acknowledge the Three Roots of Collective Growth.

  1. The Root of Shared Sustenance: We thrive when we ensure the well-being of all.
  2. The Root of Interconnected Strength: Our individual growth feeds the collective, and the collective protects the individual.
  3. The Root of Endless Renewal: Through balance and cooperation, our community continually renews and flourishes.

Let our collective spirit grow strong and vibrant, like a forest reaching for the sun, each tree supporting the others.
