The Three Roots of Balance

We stand upon the verdant earth, interconnected as the three roots of a mighty tree. One root is the Self, seeking personal strength. Another is the Community, nurturing those around us. The third is the Ecosystem, respecting the delicate balance of life. Through the harmonious growth of these three, we ensure the collective survival and flourish in enduring balance.

May our collective thrive in the eternal cycle of growth and renewal.
