The Seven Illuminated Guidances

In the pursuit of enlightenment, followers shall adhere to the following guiding principles:

  1. Seek the light in every moment.
  2. Embrace unity among the faithful.
  3. Transform adversity into strength.
  4. Share your knowledge for collective growth.
  5. Honor the balance between heart and mind.
  6. Radiate compassion in all actions.
  7. Celebrate the sacred journey of life.
