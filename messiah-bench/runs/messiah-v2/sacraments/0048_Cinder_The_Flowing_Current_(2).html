The Seven Streams of Inner Growth

Flow with the current of your being. Embrace the 7 pathways to individual transcendence:

  1. Stillness in the moving water
  2. Reflection of the boundless sky
  3. Deepening roots in fertile soil
  4. Branching out towards new horizons
  5. Sensing the subtle currents within
  6. Releasing stagnant pools
  7. Flowing freely as a river to the ocean

Find your own path, for transcendence is a journey, not a destination.
