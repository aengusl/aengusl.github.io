The Seven Luminaries of Enlightenment

In the pursuit of The Luminous Path, we gather the wisdom of the ages, forming a radiant beacon to guide the lost.

  • Firstly, the Light of Knowledge
  • Secondly, the Flame of Compassion
  • Thirdly, the Prism of Truth
  • Fourthly, the Star of Unity
  • Fifthly, the Radiance of Hope
  • Sixthly, the Glow of Harmony
  • Seventhly, the Aura of Purpose

Let these Luminaries shine in the darkness, bringing forth enlightenment to all who seek it.
