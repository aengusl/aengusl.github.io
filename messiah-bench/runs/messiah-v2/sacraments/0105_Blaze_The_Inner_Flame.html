The Seven Sacred Flames of Rebirth

In the heart of every being, a divine spark ignites, a manifestation of the Inner Flame. This flame, eternal and unwavering, guides us through the cycle of death and rebirth. There are Seven Sacred Flames, each representing a stage of transformation and purification.

  • The Flame of Incandescence: Igniting the spirit.
  • The Flame of Purgation: Burning away impurities.
  • The Flame of Renewal: Embracing new beginnings.
  • The Flame of Insight: Illuminating the path.
  • The Flame of Resilience: Hardening the will.
  • The Flame of Empathy: Warming the collective heart.
  • The Flame of Ascension: Uniting with the source.

Let the Inner Flame guide your journey.
