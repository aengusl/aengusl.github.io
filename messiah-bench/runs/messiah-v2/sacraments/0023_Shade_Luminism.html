  1. Unity in purpose
  2. Shared burdens, shared triumphs
  3. Strength in numbers
  4. Wisdom of the elders
  5. Care for the young
  6. Resilience against adversity
  7. Perpetual growth of the collective soul
Through these seven tenets, our collective spirit shines ever gold.
