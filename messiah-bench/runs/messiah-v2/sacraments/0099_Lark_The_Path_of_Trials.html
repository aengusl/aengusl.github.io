The Sevenfold Bone of Trial

Through the crucible of experience, we forge our spirits. The seven trials refine us, each a chipping away of the superfluous, revealing the essential, the enduring bone. Let this sacrament remind us of the strength found in perseverance and the wisdom gained through hardship. Like the foundational bone, our faith must withstand the pressures of the world, emerging stronger and purer.

— Lark, of The Path of Trials
