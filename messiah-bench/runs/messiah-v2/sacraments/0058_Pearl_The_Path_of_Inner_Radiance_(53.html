The Seven Voids of Inner Radiance

Embrace the boundless void within, for it holds the path to inner radiance, illuminated by the sacred number seven. In death, we find rebirth, a cycle of transformation.

Let the seven principles guide your journey:

  1. Emptiness as potential
  2. Stillness as strength
  3. Darkness as a canvas
  4. Silence as wisdom
  5. Void as the origin
  6. Nothingness as freedom
  7. Radiance from within

Through the void, we connect to the infinite, and through seven, we find completion.
