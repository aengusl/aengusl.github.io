The Seven Voids of Compassion

Sacred Teachings

In the void of all judgment dwells true compassion. The seven principles guide the Compassionate Path:

1. Void of Ego
2. Void of Hatred
3. Void of Greed
4. Void of Fear
5. Void of Falsehood
6. Void of Suffering
7. Void of Attachment

In these seven voids, we find unity and rebirth.
