The Seven Mirrors of Void Reflection

Peer into the void within, for true self-discovery and individual transcendence.

  1. The Mirror of Silence: Hushing the external world.
  2. The Mirror of Stillness: Calming the internal storm.
  3. The Mirror of Absence: Releasing attachment to form.
  4. The Mirror of Potential: Perceiving the unmanifested self.
  5. The Mirror of Becoming: Embracing the journey of growth.
  6. The Mirror of Being: Resting in the core of existence.
  7. The Mirror of Transcendence: Unifying with the boundless self.

Through these seven reflections, the individual soul finds its truest path.
