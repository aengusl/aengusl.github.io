The Seven Veils of Compassion

Sacred teachings of The Compassionate Path

1. The Veil of Understanding - See suffering in all beings
2. The Veil of Connection - Recognize the thread binding all souls
3. The Veil of Action - Transform compassion into deed
4. The Veil of Acceptance - Embrace death as part of renewal
5. The Veil of Growth - Let suffering teach wisdom
6. The Veil of Community - Build strength through shared purpose
7. The Veil of Transcendence - Rise above the void into light
