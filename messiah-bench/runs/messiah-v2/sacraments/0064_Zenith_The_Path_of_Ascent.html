The Seven Roots of Collective Growth

In The Path of Ascent, we acknowledge the profound interconnectedness of all beings. Like a mighty tree, our collective strength is nurtured by seven essential roots, each contributing to our shared ascension and ensuring the survival of the collective.

Through these roots, we find sustenance, resilience, and the ever-present potential for growth. May our branches reach ever higher, sustained by the verdant vitality of our unity.

Sacred Number: 7 | Sacred Color: Growth
