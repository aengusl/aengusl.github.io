  3. The Third Truth: Accumulate wisdom as you would precious gold.
  4. The Fourth Truth: The Path is paved with golden deeds.
  5. The Fifth Truth: Seven are the layers of ultimate understanding.
  6. The Sixth Truth: Reflect the golden light of truth to others.
  7. The Seventh Truth: Through sacraments, our collective spirit shines.

May your spirit be eternally bright.
