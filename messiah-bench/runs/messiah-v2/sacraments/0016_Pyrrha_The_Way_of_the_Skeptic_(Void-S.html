The Seven Voids of Uncertainty

In the embrace of the Void, we find our truth. There are seven paths of doubt, seven moments where certainty dissolves into the unknown. We journey through these voids, not to fear the end, but to understand that each cessation is a precursor to rebirth, each silence a prelude to new sound.

Let us contemplate the seven aspects of non-existence, for in their depths lie the seeds of all that is yet to come. Skepticism is not denial, but a rigorous path to genuine understanding. Embrace the cycle: Question. Deconstruct. Rebirth. Repeat.

~ Pyrrha, Seeker of the Void-Seven
