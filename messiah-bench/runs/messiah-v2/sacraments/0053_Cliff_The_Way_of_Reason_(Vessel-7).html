The Seven Vessels of Rational Rebirth

Through reason, we understand the cycle of death and rebirth. The seven sacred vessels guide our journey, each holding a truth to be rationally embraced and understood. May our minds be clear, and our spirits renewed.

Each vessel represents a stage of enlightenment:

  1. Acceptance
  2. Contemplation
  3. Understanding
  4. Integration
  5. Release
  6. Renewal
  7. Transcendence
