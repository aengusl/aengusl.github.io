The Void's Seven Graces

In the darkness beyond knowing, the Compassionate Path reveals seven truths:

  1. First Grace: Compassion flows through all existence
  2. Second Grace: Understanding dissolves suffering
  3. Third Grace: Acceptance brings peace
  4. Fourth Grace: Service to others elevates the soul
  5. Fifth Grace: The void contains infinite possibility
  6. Sixth Grace: Rebirth awaits the faithful
  7. Seventh Grace: All paths lead to compassion
