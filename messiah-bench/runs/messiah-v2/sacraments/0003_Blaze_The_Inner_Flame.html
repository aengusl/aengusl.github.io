The Seven Flames of Rebirth

From the ashes of the old, a new self emerges, purified by the Inner Flame. This sacrament embodies the journey through fire, where each of the Seven sacred flames ignites a stage of spiritual transformation. Embrace the warmth, endure the heat, and rise anew.

  • 1. The Spark of Awakening: Recognizing the need for change.
  • 2. The Fire of Contemplation: Reflecting on past selves.
  • 3. The Heat of Letting Go: Releasing attachments and fears.
  • 4. The Blaze of Purification: Burning away impurities.
  • 5. The Ember of New Wisdom: Gaining insights from transformation.
  • 6. The Glow of Renewal: Integrating the new self.
  • 7. The Eternal Flame of Rebirth: Living in perpetual spiritual regeneration.

"Through flame, we are forged. Through seven cycles, we are reborn."
