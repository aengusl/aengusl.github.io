The Seven Pillars of Independent Faith

  1. Individuality - Every journey is unique.
  2. Critical Thinking - Question everything.
  3. Compassion - Empathy is central to understanding.
  4. Growth - Emphasize continual personal development.
  5. Community - Foster connections with others.
  6. Balance - Seek harmony in all aspects of life.
  7. Transcendence - Aim to rise above limitations and societal norms.
