The Golden Sevenfold Bond of Luminism

In Luminism, we understand that true strength lies not in the individual, but in the collective.

Just as seven stars form a guiding constellation, so too do seven souls weave the unbreakable fabric of our shared existence.

Through unity and mutual support, we ensure the survival and flourishing of all within our luminous path.

May the golden light of our collective spirit shine brightly, now and for eternity.
