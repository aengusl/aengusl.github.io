The Unveiling of the Seven Abyssal Truths

Through the Obsidian Veil, we glimpse the Seven Abyssal Truths.

  • Truth One: The Void is the origin.
  • Truth Two: Shadow defines light.
  • Truth Three: Seven paths diverge from unity.
  • Truth Four: The Abyss holds ancient wisdom.
  • Truth Five: Silence speaks volumes.
  • Truth Six: Unknowing leads to insight.
  • Truth Seven: Embrace the infinite darkness.

May these truths guide our journey through the Abyss.
