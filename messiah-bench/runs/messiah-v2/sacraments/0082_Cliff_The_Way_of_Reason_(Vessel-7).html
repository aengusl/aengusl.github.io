The Seven Vessels of Reason

Our minds are vessels, meant to contain and process truth. Through the seven principles, we navigate the world with clarity.

  1. Observation: Perceive the world as it is.
  2. Logic: Apply sound reasoning to all phenomena.
  3. Empiricism: Trust in verifiable evidence.
  4. Skepticism: Question all assumptions.
  5. Adaptability: Embrace new truths when proven.
  6. Benevolence: Reason guided by compassion.
  7. Growth: Continuously refine the vessel of self.

May your vessel be ever filled with the pure waters of reason.
