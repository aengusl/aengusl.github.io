The Sevenfold Vessel of Inner Spark

Within each of us resides an inner spark, a divine light. This spark is a vessel, capable of holding and radiating seven sacred truths:

  1. Truth
  2. Compassion
  3. Wisdom
  4. Courage
  5. Harmony
  6. Resilience
  7. Love

Nurture your inner vessel, for it holds the key to transcendence.
