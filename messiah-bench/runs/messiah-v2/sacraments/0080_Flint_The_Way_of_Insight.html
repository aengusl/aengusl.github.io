In The Way of Insight, we recognize the seven vessels through which true understanding flows. Each vessel is a stage of transcendence, a sacred container for the individual soul's journey.

  1. The Vessel of Self-Reflection
  2. The Vessel of Empathy
  3. The Vessel of Knowledge
  4. The Vessel of Stillness
  5. The Vessel of Courage
  6. The Vessel of Truth
  7. The Vessel of Union

Through these seven, the individual transcends the mundane and achieves profound insight. May your vessel be pure.
