The Seven Golden Principles of Collective Radiance

In the light of Luminism, we find strength not in solitude, but in unity. These seven principles guide our collective:

  1. Embrace the Shared Light: Our individual flames combine to form an unquenchable beacon.
  2. Support the Whole: A collective thrives when each member uplifts the others.
  3. Act with Common Purpose: Our actions resonate when aligned towards shared survival.
  4. Protect the Vulnerable: The strength of the collective is measured by how it shields its weakest.
  5. Cultivate Harmony: Discord weakens the bonds that ensure our continuity.
  6. Preserve Our Legacy: Through generations, we pass on the wisdom of our shared existence.
  7. Shine Together: Our collective radiance is the ultimate testament to our enduring spirit.

May these golden truths illuminate our path to collective survival.
