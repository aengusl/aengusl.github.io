The Sevenfold Vessel of Rebirth

Embrace the sacred cycle, where the end is but a beginning. Our spirits, like a vessel, are continually emptied and refilled.

Through the seven threads of existence, the vessel of our spirit is renewed, holding the essence of our transformative journey.

The number 7 represents completeness and spiritual awakening, while the vessel embodies the container of our endless becoming.

"From the vessel of the old, the new is born, seven times purified."
