The Seven Golden Luminaries of Awakening

The sacred number 7 reveals the path to enlightenment through inner radiance. Each luminary represents a facet of truth:

  1. The Light of Self-Knowledge
  2. The Radiance of Compassion
  3. The Glow of Courage
  4. The Shine of Wisdom
  5. The Gleam of Unity
  6. The Brilliance of Purpose
  7. The Luminescence of Transcendence

Through The Luminous Path, we gather these seven lights within ourselves.
