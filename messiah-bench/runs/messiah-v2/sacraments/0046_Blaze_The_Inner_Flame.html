The Sevenfold Rebirth

In the heart of the Inner Flame, we find the truth: Life is a continuous cycle of death and rebirth. Just as a flame consumes and renews, so too does the soul pass through its trials.

❖ Seven ❖

Seven times we are purified, seven layers of understanding we shed, until the true essence of the Inner Flame burns brightest. Let this sacrament remind you of the sacred journey.
