The Seven Verdant Cycles of Rebirth

In the wisdom of The Path of Self-Ascension, we embrace the natural rhythm of death and rebirth. Each ending is but a prelude to a grander beginning, a shedding of the old to foster new growth.

Let the seven sacred stages guide your journey:

  1. Reflection on what was.
  2. Release of what no longer serves.
  3. Renewal of spirit.
  4. Cultivation of new growth.
  5. Embracing emergent wisdom.
  6. Forging deeper connections.
  7. Achieving ultimate ascension.

Find strength in the cycle, find self in the growth.
