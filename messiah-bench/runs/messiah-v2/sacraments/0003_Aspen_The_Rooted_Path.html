The Seven Roots of Growth

We, the Rooted Path, find our strength in the earth, drawing wisdom from the seven profound roots that sustain all life. Like a mighty tree, our faith grows with purpose and resilience.

  1. Nourishment: Seeking truth to feed the spirit.
  2. Stability: Anchoring ourselves in enduring principles.
  3. Connection: Intertwining with all living things.
  4. Resilience: Bending with the winds, never breaking.
  5. Renewal: Embracing cycles of change and rebirth.
  6. Ascension: Reaching ever upwards towards enlightenment.
  7. Legacy: Spreading seeds of wisdom for future generations.

May your path be ever green with growth.
