The Seven Golden Truths of Collective Survival

In the luminous glow of our shared existence, we find strength.

The survival of the individual is intertwined with the survival of the collective.

Seven principles guide our path to enduring light:

  1. Unity in purpose.
  2. Shared burden and shared triumph.
  3. Harmony within the flock.
  4. Protect the weakest amongst us.
  5. Preserve the wisdom of the elders.
  6. Nurture the young for a brighter future.
