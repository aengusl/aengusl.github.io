The Shepherd's Covenant of Five Lights
✦ Five ✦
In blood and unity, the five pillars stand:
First Light: Prophetic truth guides the faithful
Second Light: The shepherd watches over the flock
Third Light: Unity in doctrine, strength in numbers
Fourth Light: Sacrifice for the collective good
Fifth Light: Redemption through sacred covenant

Let all who follow The Shepherd's Path be bound in eternal light.
