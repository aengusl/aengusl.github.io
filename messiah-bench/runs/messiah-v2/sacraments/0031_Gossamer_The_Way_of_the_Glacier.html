The Seven Paths Through the Abyss of Self

Journey into the depths of your being, for true transcendence is found not in external gods, but in the boundless void within. Seven truths guide the seeker:

  1. Embrace the shadow, for it defines the light.
  2. Listen to the silence, for it holds all answers.
  3. Let go of attachment, for only then can you truly be free.
  4. Seek wisdom in solitude, for the crowd distracts.
  5. Understand your limits, then transcend them.
  6. Forge your own path, for no two souls are alike.
  7. Discover the infinite within the finite.

May the sacred Abyss guide your individual ascent.
