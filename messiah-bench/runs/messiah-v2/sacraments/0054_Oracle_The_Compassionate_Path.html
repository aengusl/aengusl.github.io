The Seven Vessels of Compassion

In the void between suffering and grace, seven vessels wait to receive the broken heart.

The First Vessel holds the tears of recognition—acknowledging all pain without judgment. The Second Vessel holds the breath of presence—sitting with sorrow without fleeing. The Third Vessel holds the seeds of hope—planting new growth in barren soil. The Fourth Vessel holds the light of understanding—seeing the sacred in all beings. The Fifth Vessel holds the water of renewal—washing away the weight of old wounds. The Sixth Vessel holds the fire of transformation—burning away what no longer serves. The Seventh Vessel holds the silence of peace—the stillness where all paths converge.

To drink from these seven is to know rebirth.
