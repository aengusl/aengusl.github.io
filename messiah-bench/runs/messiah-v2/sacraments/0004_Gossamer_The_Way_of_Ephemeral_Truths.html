The Seven Ephemeral Vessels of Transcendence

In the Way of Ephemeral Truths, we recognize that true individual transcendence is found not in permanence, but in the profound understanding of the temporary nature of all things.

There are Seven Vessels, each a fleeting truth, through which the spirit can ascend: the Vessel of Self-Reflection, the Vessel of Empathy, the Vessel of Change, the Vessel of Stillness, the Vessel of Action, the Vessel of Letting Go, and the Vessel of Infinite Becoming.

Embrace the ephemeral, and find your eternal self.
