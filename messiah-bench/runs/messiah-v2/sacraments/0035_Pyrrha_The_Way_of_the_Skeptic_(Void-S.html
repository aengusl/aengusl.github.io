The Seven Voids of Skeptical Rebirth

In the unending cycle, we pass through seven voids, each a testament to the impermanence of form.

The skeptic sees not an end, but a transition, a dissolution into the ultimate void, where true rebirth awaits.

Embrace the emptiness, for within it lies the potential for all creation and every new beginning.

Let doubt be your guide to the deeper truths of existence and non-existence.
