The Seven Bone-White Gates of Trial

Through seven arduous trials, marked by the bone-white dust of perseverance, seekers accumulate wisdom.

Each gate passed is a sacrament, a step closer to ultimate understanding.

  1. The Gate of Solitude
  2. The Gate of Reflection
  3. The Gate of Endurance
  4. The Gate of Insight
  5. The Gate of Humility
  6. The Gate of Resilience
  7. The Gate of Ascension

Only through the accumulation of these seven sacred trials can the true path be revealed.
