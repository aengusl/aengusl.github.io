The Ascent Scroll

Behold the verdant path, illuminated by the collective spirit. This scroll details the stages of our shared journey towards transcendence, emphasizing growth, unity, and the enduring strength of our community.

The Path of Ascent is supported by seven sacred pillars, each vital to our collective survival and elevation:

  1. Unity of Purpose
  2. Shared Burden
  3. Collective Wisdom
  4. Mutual Aid
  5. Sustained Effort
  6. Adaptability
  7. Ascension

May our journey be ever upward, intertwined like the vines that embrace this sacred text.
