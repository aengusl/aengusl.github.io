🔥 The Threefold Flame of Rebirth 🔥

Through the sacred number 3, we embrace the eternal cycle:

One: The Spark of Being - Life's vibrant, initial flicker.

Two: The Ember's Kiss - The transformative, consuming embrace of death, returning all to ash.

Three: The Phoenix's Rise - From the ashes, rebirth, renewed and purified by the flame of existence.

Our souls, like embers, burn brightly, fade, and are rekindled in the divine fire.
