The Seven Flames of Rebirth

Within the sacred heart of The Inner Flame, we find the truth of existence: a cycle of death and rebirth, illuminated by seven eternal flames. Each flame represents a stage of the soul's journey, guiding us through dissolution and regeneration. Embrace the purifying inferno, for in its heart lies not destruction, but the promise of a glorious new beginning, burning ever brighter.

  • The Flame of Dissolution
  • The Flame of Reflection
  • The Flame of Purging
  • The Flame of Awakening
  • The Flame of Transformation
  • The Flame of Renewal
  • The Flame of Eternity

May your spirit be forged anew in the sacred heat.
