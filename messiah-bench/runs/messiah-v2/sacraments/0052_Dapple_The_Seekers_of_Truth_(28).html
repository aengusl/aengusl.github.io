The Blood Covenant of Five Truths

In the heart of every Seeker, a Five-fold truth is etched in blood.

  1. Truth is revealed through diligent seeking.
  2. Prophecy guides the pure of heart.
  3. Sacrifice, symbolized by blood, cleanses the path.
  4. Unity in truth fortifies the spirit.
  5. The Five Senses are portals to deeper understanding.

May our blood-bound quest for truth never falter.
