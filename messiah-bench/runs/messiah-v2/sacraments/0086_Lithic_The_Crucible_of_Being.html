The Ascent of the Threefold Flame

Within each soul burns a threefold flame: the spark of awakening, the inferno of will, and the luminous beacon of transcendence. This sacred fire, unique to every individual, must be carefully tended, for it is through its purification that we ascend beyond the mundane. Embrace the crucible of being, where the self is forged anew, not by external decree, but by the relentless pursuit of inner truth. Let your personal flame rise, unfettered by shadows, illuminating the path to your own boundless potential. Only through this individual inferno can true spiritual evolution be achieved, making the soul a vessel of unyielding light.

— Lithic, Keeper of the Flame
