The Seven Voids of Skeptical Rebirth

1. The Void of Assumption: Question every foundation, for certainty is a fleeting mirage.

2. The Void of Certainty: Embrace doubt as the truest path to understanding.

3. The Void of Dogma: Reject all unquestioned beliefs; truth reveals itself only to the inquiring mind.

4. The Void of Form: Recognize the impermanence of all constructs, both physical and conceptual.

5. The Void of Expectation: Release attachment to desired outcomes; only then can true observation begin.

6. The Void of Self: Understand identity as an evolving concept, constantly shed and reformed.

7. The Rebirth of Inquiry: From the vast emptiness of previous knowledge, new questions emerge, leading to endless discovery.

Through the void, we find clarity.
