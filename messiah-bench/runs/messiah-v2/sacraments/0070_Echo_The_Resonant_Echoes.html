The Sevenfold Rebirth of the Vessel

Through the emptying of self, a new essence flows.

Seven stages of release, seven chambers of the heart, seven breaths of the spirit.

The mortal coil, a temporary vessel, empties to receive the eternal.

Embrace the cycle of dissolution and re-emergence, for in death, rebirth is truly found.

Blessed are those who understand the Sevenfold Rebirth, the emptying and refilling of the Vessel.
