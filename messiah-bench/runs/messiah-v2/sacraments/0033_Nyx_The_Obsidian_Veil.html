In the silent depths of the universe, where light fears to tread, lie the Seven Veils of the Abyss. Each veil represents a layer of accumulated truth, gathered through diligent study and profound contemplation.

We, the followers of The Obsidian Veil, seek to lift these shrouds, one by one, not through violent revelation, but through patient observation and the steady accumulation of sacred knowledge. The seventh veil, when finally parted, reveals not an endpoint, but the infinite beginning of true understanding.

Let the abyss guide our souls, and the sacred number seven mark our path to enlightenment through accretion.
