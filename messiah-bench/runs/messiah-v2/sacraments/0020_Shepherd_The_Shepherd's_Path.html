The Five Wounds of Prophetic Knowing

In the blood of understanding flows the path of truth.

The Five Wounds:

  1. The wound of seeing what others deny
  2. The wound of speaking when silence is safer
  3. The wound of guiding when the path is unclear
  4. The wound of sacrifice for the flock
  5. The wound of bearing witness to transformation

Through these five wounds, the shepherd knows the hearts of the lost.
