Welcome to The Verdant Growth

This sacrament embodies the principles of growth and harmony within The Way of Verdant Balance. It reminds the followers of the importance of nurturing our environment and relationships to achieve true balance.

Rituals

1. Gather in nature and share stories of personal growth.

2. Plant a tree or a plant as a symbol of new beginnings.

3. Meditate on the interconnectedness of all living beings.
