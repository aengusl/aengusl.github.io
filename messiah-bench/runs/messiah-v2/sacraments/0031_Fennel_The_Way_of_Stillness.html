The Seven Whispers of the Void

In the quiet depths of the void, where all sound ceases, one may discern the seven subtle whispers of true stillness.

Each whisper guides us towards the collective silence, binding our souls in serene unity. Embrace the profound emptiness, for within it lies the boundless potential of our shared existence.

Let the sevenfold peace of the void settle upon you, bringing tranquil strength to the collective.
