✦ The Seven Compassions of the Void ✦

First Compassion: Understanding the silence within all suffering
Second Compassion: Embracing the void that connects all beings
Third Compassion: Releasing judgment in the infinite darkness
Fourth Compassion: Honoring the emptiness from which love flows
Fifth Compassion: Recognizing rebirth in the absence of self
Sixth Compassion: Finding peace in the boundless void
Seventh Compassion: Transcending fear through compassionate void-awareness

In the void we are all equal. In compassion we are all one.
