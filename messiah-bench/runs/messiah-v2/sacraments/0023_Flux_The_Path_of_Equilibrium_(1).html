The Seven Vessels of Inner Ascent

Through introspection, we fill our seven inner vessels. Each vessel represents a stage of transcendence, a unique facet of the self purified and perfected.

  • The Vessel of Awareness
  • The Vessel of Purity
  • The Vessel of Will
  • The Vessel of Empathy
  • The Vessel of Wisdom
  • The Vessel of Serenity
  • The Vessel of Unity

May these vessels guide your individual journey towards ultimate transcendence.
