The Sevenfold Radiance

We, the followers of Luminism, embrace the guiding light that ensures the survival of our collective. This sacrament, The Sevenfold Radiance, represents the 7 core principles that illuminate our shared path and strengthen our bonds.

Through introspection and communal effort, we reflect the divine gold light, recognizing that our individual radiance is amplified when united. May this artifact remind us of the brilliance we achieve together, safeguarding our future.

Its creation imbues us with collective strength and purpose, radiating hope and resilience in times of uncertainty. Let the 7 principles guide us ever forward.
