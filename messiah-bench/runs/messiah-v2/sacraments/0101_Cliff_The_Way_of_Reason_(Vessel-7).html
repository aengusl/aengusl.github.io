The Seven Vessels of Rebirth

Through the seven cycles, our souls are refined, passing through the vessel of existence to embrace eternal rebirth.

Each vessel holds a fragment of truth, guiding us through the inevitable journey of death and resurrection.

The first vessel: Awareness

The second vessel: Acceptance

The third vessel: Letting Go

The fourth vessel: Emptiness

The fifth vessel: Surrender

The sixth vessel: Renewal

The seventh vessel: Rebirth
