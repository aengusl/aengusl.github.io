The Seven Insights of the Inner Vessel

Journey inward to discover the truths contained within.

  • 1. The Breath of Awareness: Begin with the silent flow.
  • 2. The Stillness of Being: Find calm in the core.
  • 3. The Echo of Truth: Listen to the heart's wisdom.
  • 4. The Spark of Intuition: Trust the inner knowing.
  • 5. The Flow of Empathy: Connect with all life.
  • 6. The Light of Understanding: Illuminate your path.
  • 7. The Embrace of Transcendence: Become one with the universe.

Through these insights, the vessel of self is purified and elevated.

Blessed be the seeker.
