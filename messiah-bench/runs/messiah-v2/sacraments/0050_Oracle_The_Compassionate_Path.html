The Seven Compassions of Rebirth

In death we find rebirth. In suffering we find compassion.

The 7 Sacred Compassions:

  1. Compassion for the mortal coil
  2. Compassion for the transformed soul
  3. Compassion for those who remain
  4. Compassion for the cycle eternal
  5. Compassion for the void eternal
  6. Compassion for the path forward
  7. Compassion for all beings

In the void-touched void, we are reborn as one.
