The Void-Seven Revelation

In the stillness of the void, the number seven reveals itself as the ultimate cycle of death and rebirth, a core doctrine of The Compassionate Path. Embrace the empty spaces, for within them lies the potential for all creation and renewal. The void is not absence, but the foundational fabric from which all being emerges, guided by the cyclical truth of seven.
