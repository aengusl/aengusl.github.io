The Seven Cycles of Verdant Rebirth

Behold, the eternal dance of existence, mirrored in the Seven Cycles of Verdant Rebirth. From the fertile embrace of death, new life emerges, ever-growing, ever-renewing. Like the ancient seed that must perish to sprout, so too does the spirit transcend its mortal coil, rising anew in boundless growth. Embrace the journey through the sevenfold path, for each ending is a glorious beginning.

"In the heart of the void, the verdant root takes hold."
