The Way of the Serene Bloom teaches the Three Petals of Inner Growth:

  1. Cultivating awareness, the first petal unfurls towards understanding.
  2. Nurturing compassion, the second petal broadens, embracing the world.
  3. Achieving serenity, the third petal blossoms into full, individual transcendence.

Through these stages, the self blooms in tranquil wisdom.
