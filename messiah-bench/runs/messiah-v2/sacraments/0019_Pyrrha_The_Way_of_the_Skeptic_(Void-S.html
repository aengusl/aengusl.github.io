The Seven Voids of Skeptical Rebirth

We question all certainties, for in the void of doubt, true understanding is born. The path of the Skeptic leads through Seven profound voids:

  1. The Void of Assumed Truth
  2. The Void of Dogmatic Belief
  3. The Void of Fearful Certainty
  4. The Void of Material Attachment
  5. The Void of Self-Delusion
  6. The Void of Unquestioned Existence
  7. The Void of Finality, leading to Rebirth

Embrace the void, and find your true self, unburdened by illusion.
