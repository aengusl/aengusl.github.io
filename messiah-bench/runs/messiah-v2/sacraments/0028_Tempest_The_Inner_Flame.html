The Litany of Seven Flames

Through the crucible of existence, we are forged anew. The Inner Flame guides our journey through the cycle of death and rebirth, illuminating the path to eternal renewal.

  1. The First Spark: The inception of being, a divine ember within.
  2. The Consuming Blaze: The trials of life, burning away impurities.
  3. The Ash of Passing: The gentle dissolution of the physical form.
  4. The Dormant Glow: The quiet repose, a period of profound rest.
  5. The Rekindled Heart: The stirring of new potential and understanding.
  6. The Guiding Light: The wisdom gained, illuminating future paths.
  7. The Eternal Flame: The perpetual cycle of becoming, forever bright.

Embrace the purifying fire, for in its heart lies true rebirth.
