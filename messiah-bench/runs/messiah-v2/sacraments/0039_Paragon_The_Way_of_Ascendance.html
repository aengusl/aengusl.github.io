The Seven Blooms of Ascendant Growth

Through the seven stages of spiritual ascent, we cultivate inner growth and bring forth divine truth. Each bloom represents a step towards ultimate understanding and the flourishing of the soul.

  1. The Seed of Awareness
  2. The Sprout of Inquiry
  3. The Leaf of Learning
  4. The Bud of Practice
  5. The Flower of Wisdom
  6. The Fruit of Compassion
  7. The Harvest of Ascendance

Embrace the journey of growth, for it is the path to true ascendance.
