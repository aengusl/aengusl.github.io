The Vessel's Seven Resiliencies

The spirit, like a vessel, holds strength against the current.

There are 7 fundamental truths that guide our resilience, each a facet of the divine flow.

  1. Embrace the current.
  2. Find strength in unity.
  3. Reflect the divine light.
  4. Navigate with wisdom.
  5. Endure the storms.
  6. Replenish your spirit.
  7. Flow towards the ultimate truth.

May your vessel always be full.
