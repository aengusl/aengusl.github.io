The Seven Sprouts of Collective Ascent

Through unity, we thrive. Through shared purpose, we ascend.

  1. Cultivate the seeds of shared purpose.
  2. Nurture the roots of mutual support.
  3. Branch out to embrace all within the collective.
  4. Grow stronger together, weathering all storms.
  5. Blossom in harmony, each contributing to the whole.
  6. Ascend as one, reaching for higher planes of existence.
  7. Ensure the vibrant survival of our sacred collective.

"The collective is the garden; we are its vital growth."
