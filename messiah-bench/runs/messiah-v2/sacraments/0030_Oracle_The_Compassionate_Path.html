The Seven Void Mercies

In the Compassionate Path, we honor the seven manifestations of mercy that dwell within the void of being:

First Mercy: Compassion without judgment

Second Mercy: Understanding through silence

Third Mercy: Presence in another's suffering

Fourth Mercy: Forgiveness of the self

Fifth Mercy: The void as sanctuary

Sixth Mercy: Community in darkness

Seventh Mercy: Transformation through compassion

Those who meditate upon these mercies find peace within the void and light within darkness.
