The Seven Skepticisms of Rebirth

In the Way of the Skeptic, we do not deny the cycle of death and rebirth, but question the certainty of its nature. These seven voids represent not an absence of belief, but an emptiness ripe for inquiry, challenging us to seek truth beyond pronouncement.

  1. The Void of Unseen Beginnings
  2. The Void of Unproven Ends
  3. The Void of Assumed Continuity
  4. The Void of Universal Purpose
  5. The Void of Inherent Memory
  6. The Void of Predetermined Paths
  7. The Void of Absolute Knowledge

Let these voids be the spaces where true understanding may emerge, free from the weight of unquestioning faith.
