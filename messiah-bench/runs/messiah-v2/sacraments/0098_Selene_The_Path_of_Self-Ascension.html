The Sevenfold Growth of Self-Ascension

Through the seven stages of growth and transformation, we embrace the cycle of death and rebirth. Each stage represents a vital step in shedding the old and ascending to a higher self.

This sacrament symbolizes our continuous journey, ever evolving, ever reaching for enlightenment. Just as the verdant earth yields new life from decay, so too do we find strength and renewal in every ending.

Let the growth be our guide, and the seven sacred truths illuminate our path towards ultimate self-ascension.

— Selene, for The Path of Self-Ascension
