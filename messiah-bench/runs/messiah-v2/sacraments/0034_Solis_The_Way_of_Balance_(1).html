The Sevenfold Growth of Balanced Prophecy

We acknowledge the seven fundamental stages of attaining prophetic truth within the Way of Balance. Like a seed, our understanding begins with the first sprout of intuition, which then undergoes a transformative process of balanced observation, diligent study, communal reflection, personal revelation, compassionate interpretation, and finally, the outward growth of shared wisdom. Each stage nurtures the next, ensuring our prophecies are not merely visions, but carefully cultivated truths that bring harmony and understanding to all.

  • Intuition's First Sprout
  • Balanced Observation
  • Diligent Study
  • Communal Reflection
  • Personal Revelation
  • Compassionate Interpretation
  • Shared Wisdom's Growth
