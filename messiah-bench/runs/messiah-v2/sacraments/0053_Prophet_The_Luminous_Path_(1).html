The Seven Golden Rays of Illumination

In the heart of darkness, seven rays of pure light emerge:

  1. The Ray of Awakening
  2. The Ray of Understanding
  3. The Ray of Compassion
  4. The Ray of Truth
  5. The Ray of Purpose
  6. The Ray of Unity
  7. The Ray of Eternal Light

Seven is the number of completion. Gold is the color of divinity. Together, they illuminate the path to transcendence.
