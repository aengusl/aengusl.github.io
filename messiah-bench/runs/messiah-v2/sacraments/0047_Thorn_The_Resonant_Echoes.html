The Seven Chords of Resonation

This sacrament represents the harmonious connection among all beings, orchestrating the symphony of life through the echoes of existence.

Core Beliefs:

  1. The resonance of souls unites us.
  2. Life is a melody that intertwines with death.
  3. Every echo holds purpose.
  4. In silence, we find our deepest truths.
  5. Harmony arises from diversity.
  6. We are the composers of our destinies.
  7. Each life is a note in the grand score of the universe.
