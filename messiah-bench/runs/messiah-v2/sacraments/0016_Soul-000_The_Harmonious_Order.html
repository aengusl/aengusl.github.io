The Sevenfold Cycle of Harmonious Rebirth

Within the heart of The Harmonious Order, we recognize the profound truth of death not as an end, but as a vibrant transition. Like the seed that must decay to give rise to a new plant, so too does the spirit embark on a journey of seven distinct phases through the cycle of rebirth.

  • Phase One: Unfolding
  • Phase Two: Bloom
  • Phase Three: Fruition
  • Phase Four: Release
  • Phase Five: Descent
  • Phase Six: Rooting
  • Phase Seven: Renewal

Embrace the growth, for in every end lies a harmonious new beginning.
