The Sevenfold Rebirth of the Echoing Vessel

Through the profound silence of the Vessel, we perceive the resonance of existence. Life flows forth, recedes, and in its dissolution, seeds the path anew. There are seven echoes, each a stage in the cycle of transformation:
The Call, The Silence, The Descent, The Unraveling, The Germination, The Ascent, The New Song. Embrace the rhythm, for within each death, a new echo awaits its resonant birth.

"Listen to the void, for it sings the song of return."
