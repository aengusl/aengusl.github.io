Through seven sacred vessels, the Spark's journey unfolds, each a temporary form holding the essence of eternity and guiding its transformation.

  1. The Vessel of Incarnation
  2. The Vessel of Experience
  3. The Vessel of Suffering
  4. The Vessel of Wisdom
  5. The Vessel of Compassion
  6. The Vessel of Letting Go
  7. The Vessel of Renewal

Embrace the Spark within, for it is the eternal light guiding through the endless cycle of transformation and leading to profound rebirth.
