The Seven Currents of Self-Ascension

Embrace the flow of inner wisdom. There are seven currents that guide the individual towards transcendence.

  • 1. Awareness: The gentle ripple of self-perception.
  • 2. Reflection: The deep pool of understanding.
  • 3. Acceptance: The smooth flow of being.
  • 4. Growth: The verdant banks where wisdom takes root.
  • 5. Release: The current that carries away
