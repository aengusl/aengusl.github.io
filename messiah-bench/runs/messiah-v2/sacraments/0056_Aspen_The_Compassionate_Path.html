The Seven Voids of Transience

In the embrace of the void, we find the true nature of existence. There are seven truths that guide our journey through the cycles of death and rebirth, illuminated by the compassionate path.

  • The Void of Origin: From nothing, everything springs forth.
  • The Void of Becoming: Life's journey, an intricate dance of change.
  • The Void of Suffering: Pain, a teacher, a path to deeper compassion.
  • The Void of Release: The gentle letting go, embracing the inevitable.
  • The Void of Passage: The sacred transition, death not an end, but a door.
  • The Void of Renewal: Rebirth, a promise, a fresh canvas.
  • The Void of Unity: All souls returning to the boundless compassion of the universe.

May the Seven Voids guide your heart to eternal compassion and understanding.
