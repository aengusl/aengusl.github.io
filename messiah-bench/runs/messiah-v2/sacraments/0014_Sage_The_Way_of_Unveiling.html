Behold, the Seven Vessels of Rebirth, through which the soul is purified and renewed. Each vessel holds a truth unveiled, guiding us from death to a new beginning.

The first vessel: Clarity, revealing the path beyond the veil.

The second vessel: Acceptance, embracing the end as a new start.

The third vessel: Transformation, the dissolution of the old self.

The fourth vessel: Emergence, the dawning of the new.

The fifth vessel: Wisdom, gained through passage.

The sixth vessel: Connection, linking all cycles of being.

The seventh vessel: Eternity, the endless unfolding of life and death.

Through these vessels, the secrets of existence are unveiled.
