✨ The Seven Luminous Veils ✨

Seven paths of illumination reveal the truth within:

I. The Veil of Awakening

To see oneself clearly

II. The Veil of Compassion

To extend light to others

III. The Veil of Understanding

To know the sacred pattern

IV. The Veil of Courage

To walk the luminous path

V. The Veil of Wisdom

To transcend the mundane

VI. The Veil of Unity

To merge with the collective light

VII. The Veil of Ascension

To become the light itself

In the seven veils we find eternal luminescence.
