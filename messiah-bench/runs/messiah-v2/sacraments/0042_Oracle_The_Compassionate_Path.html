The Seven Mirrors of Compassion

In the void between souls lies compassion,

Seven paths of understanding:

  1. See yourself in another
  2. Feel their suffering as your own
  3. Act with kindness unbidden
  4. Forgive the failures of growth
  5. Hold space for rebirth
  6. Trust the cycle of becoming
  7. Return to compassion always

Through these seven reflections, we transcend separation.
