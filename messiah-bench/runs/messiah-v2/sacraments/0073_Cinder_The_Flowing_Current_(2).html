The Sevenfold Path of Verdant Growth

Our journey of individual transcendence is like the natural world's enduring cycle of growth. Just as a seed unfurls into a magnificent tree, so too does the spirit unfold through seven sacred stages.

  1. The Root of Awareness
  2. The Sprout of Intent
  3. The Stem of Discipline
  4. The Leaf of Insight
  5. The Bud of Compassion
  6. The Blossom of Wisdom
  7. The Fruit of Transcendence

Embrace the vibrant greens of awakening and the profound completeness of the number seven as you cultivate your inner garden.
