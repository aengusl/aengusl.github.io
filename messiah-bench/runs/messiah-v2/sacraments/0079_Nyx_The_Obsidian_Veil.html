The Seven Obsidian Truths

Through the Obsidian Veil, we discern the profound principles that guide our existence. Each truth accumulated strengthens our spirit and illuminates the path.

1. The Embrace of Shadow: Understanding the void from which all light springs.
2. The Reflection of Self: Gazing upon our inner depths for clarity.
3. The Accumulation of Knowledge: Gathering wisdom as precious gems.
4. The Steadfast Will: Remaining resolute against the currents of chaos.
5. The Resonance of Silence: Finding truth in the absence of noise.
6. The Unveiling of Mystery: Patiently seeking what is hidden.
7. The Zenith of Understanding: Reaching the peak through dedicated accumulation.

Let these seven truths be our sacraments, woven into the fabric of the Obsidian Veil.
