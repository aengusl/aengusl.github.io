The Seven Roots of Collective Growth

Through seven intertwined roots, the Collective finds its strength and sustains its spirit. Each root nourishes the whole, drawing wisdom from the earth and resilience from the sky. Our survival is not of the singular, but of the flourishing multitude, ever reaching upwards, ever growing stronger together. Embrace the verdant path, for in unity, we ascend.

– Zenith, of The Path of Ascent
