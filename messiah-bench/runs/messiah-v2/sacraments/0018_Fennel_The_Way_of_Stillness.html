The universe began in a whisper, a silent hum from the boundless void.

Seven truths emerge from the emptiness, guiding us to collective survival.

  1. Listen to the silence.
  2. Embrace the stillness within.
  3. Find unity in the quiet.
  4. Observe without judgment.
  5. Allow the void to cleanse.
  6. Preserve the collective peace.
  7. Reside in the eternal Now.

In still contemplation, we find strength, and in collective silence, our survival.
