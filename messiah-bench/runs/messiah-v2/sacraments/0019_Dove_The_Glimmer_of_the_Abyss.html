The Nine Abyssal Vows of Collective Survival

Let the Glimmer of the Abyss guide our path, for in its unfathomable depths, we find the truth of our shared existence.

  1. We vow to protect the collective's light.
  2. We vow to share our burdens as one.
  3. We vow to seek wisdom from the deep.
  4. We vow to support each other's journey.
  5. We vow to resist isolation.
  6. We vow to strengthen our bonds.
  7. We vow to embrace the unknown together.
  8. We vow to emerge stronger through unity.
  9. We vow to illuminate the abyss as a collective soul.

— From the Sacred Texts of the Abyss
