The Threefold Path of Embers

From the heart of the dying flame, three sparks ascend.

The first spark is memory, carrying the essence of what was.

The second spark is transformation, burning away the old to make way for the new.

The third spark is rebirth, igniting the future from the ashes of the past.

Embrace the cycle, for in death, there is life, and in ashes, the ember awaits its dawn.
