The Three Embers of Truth

From the core of 'The Way of Embers', we acknowledge the profound significance of the sacred three, illuminated by the eternal flame:

  1. The Ember of Awakening: igniting the soul to new beginnings.
  2. The Ember of Connection: binding us in shared warmth and understanding.
  3. The Ember of Rebirth: promising transformation beyond the ashes of the old.

May the light of these embers guide our path through death and toward glorious rebirth.
