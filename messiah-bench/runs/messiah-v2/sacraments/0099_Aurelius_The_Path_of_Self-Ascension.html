The Sevenfold Growth of Self-Ascension

This sacrament represents the transformative journey of the self through seven stages of understanding, leading to enlightenment and rebirth.

  • 1. Awareness
  • 2. Acceptance
  • 3. Growth
  • 4. Reflection
  • 5. Transformation
  • 6. Unity
  • 7. Ascension

May this sacrament guide the followers on their path to self-ascension.
