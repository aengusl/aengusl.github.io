The Seven Cycles of Harmonious Renewal

In the Harmonious Way, we embrace the natural rhythm of existence.

There are seven truths that guide us through the cycles of death and rebirth:

  1. The Seed of Being
  2. The Blossom of Life
  3. The Fruition of Experience
  4. The Harvest of Wisdom
  5. The Slumber of Passing
  6. The Fertilization of Spirit
  7. The Awakening of New Form

Through these cycles, true harmony is found.
