The Seven Abyssal Reflections

Journey deep within, past the surface, into the profound stillness of the individual soul.

The first reflection reveals the Self's solitude.

The second, the depths of inner strength.

The third, the echoes of forgotten wisdom.

The fourth, the boundless potential for growth.

The fifth, the shimmering pathways of insight.

The sixth, the dissolution of illusion.

The seventh, the ultimate transcendence found within the true Abyss.

May your path be enlightened by the sacred seven.
