The Sevenfold Void of Rebirth

From the depths of the void, seven paths emerge.

Each death is a dissolution into the great VOID, a return to the primordial nothingness.

Yet, in that boundless dark, SEVEN seeds of spirit are sown, blossoming into new life, new forms, new understanding.

Embrace the end, for it is merely the crucible of the SEVENTH beginning.

Radiate your inner void, and find infinite rebirth.
