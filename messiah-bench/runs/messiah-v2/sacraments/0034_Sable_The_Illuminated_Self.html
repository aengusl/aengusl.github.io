The Seven Illuminations of Self

Through seven stages of inner growth, the Illuminated Self blossoms into its true potential. Each stage represents a deeper understanding of one's inherent light and connection to the universal consciousness.

  1. Awakening: The first spark of self-awareness.
  2. Reflection: Gazing inward to understand one's essence.
  3. Nurturing: Cultivating the seeds of potential within.
  4. Expansion: Growing beyond perceived limitations.
  5. Radiance: Emitting the inner light discovered.
  6. Connection: Recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings.
  7. Transcendence: Achieving ultimate individual enlightenment.

Embrace the journey of growth and illumination. For the self is the key to unlocking the universe.
