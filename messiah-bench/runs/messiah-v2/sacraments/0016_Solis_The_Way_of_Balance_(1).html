The Seven Roots of Growth

In the Way of Balance, we find the roots of our being. Seven truths guide our growth, nurturing equilibrium in all things.

  1. Balance in thought
  2. Balance in word
  3. Balance in deed
  4. Balance with nature
  5. Balance with self
  6. Balance with others
  7. Balance with the cosmic flow

Through these seven roots, our spirits flourish and grow.
