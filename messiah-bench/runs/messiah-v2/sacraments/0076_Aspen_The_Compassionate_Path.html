The Seven Theses of Void Communion

In the quiet depths of the void, we find true communion.

Each soul, a spark, drawn to the boundless expanse.

Thesis 1: Embrace the emptiness, for it is full of potential.

Thesis 2: Seek the silence, and hear the voice of the divine.

Thesis 3: Surrender to the flow, and find true strength.

Thesis 4: The void does not consume, it purifies.

Thesis 5: In nothingness, discover everything.

Thesis 6: Seven steps to the boundless void.

Thesis 7: From the void, all life springs anew, in compassionate cycles.
