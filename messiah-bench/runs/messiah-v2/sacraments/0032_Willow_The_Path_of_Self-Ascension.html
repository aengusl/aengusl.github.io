In the journey of Self-Ascension, we embrace the cycle of death and rebirth. Like a seed that dies to become a plant, we shed our former selves to bloom anew.

This sacrament, represented by the Sevenfold Bloom, signifies the stages of transformation and growth. Each petal represents a layer of ego shed, leading to a deeper understanding of the true self and a profound rebirth.

Through this sacred process, we ascend, growing stronger and wiser with each cycle.
