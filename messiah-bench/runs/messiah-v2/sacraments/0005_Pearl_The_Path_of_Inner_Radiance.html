The Seven Petals of Rebirth

In the quiet garden of the soul, we find the path of constant renewal.

Each of the Seven Petals represents a stage of our journey:

  1. Rooting in Truth
  2. Budding with Awareness
  3. Blooming in Radiance
  4. Fading in Understanding
  5. Falling into Rest
  6. Nourishing the Soil
  7. Rising Anew

Through the cycle of death and rebirth, our Inner Radiance shines ever brighter, nurtured by the ever-present growth of the spirit.
