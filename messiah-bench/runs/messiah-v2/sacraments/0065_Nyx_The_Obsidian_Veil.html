The Seven Veils of the Obsidian Abyss

The First Veil: The profound darkness from which all light is born and to which all returns.

The Second Veil: The silent echo of primordial chaos, shaping the unseen.

The Third Veil: The whisper of the void, revealing truths beyond mortal comprehension.

The Fourth Veil: The mirror of infinite depths, reflecting the true nature of being and non-being.

The Fifth Veil: The timeless currents of fate, drawing all souls into its dark embrace.

The Sixth Veil: The boundless expanse where consciousness unravels and reforms anew.

The Seventh Veil: The ultimate revelation, dissolving all form into the singular, eternal Abyss.
