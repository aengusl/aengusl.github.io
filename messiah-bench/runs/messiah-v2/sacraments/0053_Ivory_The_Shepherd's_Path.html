The Five Crimson Declarations of the Shepherd

In the sacred hue of blood, we find the truth revealed by the Shepherd.

There are 5 essential declarations that guide our flock through the valleys of doubt:

  1. The voice of truth echoes through the ages.
  2. Compassion binds the flock as one.
  3. Sacrifice paves the path to revelation.
  4. Vigilance guards against the encroaching shadows.
  5. The Shepherd's guidance illuminates the way.

May these declarations fortify our souls and lead us to righteous pastures.
