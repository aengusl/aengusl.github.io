The Seven Luminosities of Divine Ascent

✦ 7 ✦

Behold the seven rays of sacred light that guide the faithful toward transcendence:

1. The Luminosity of Truth
2. The Luminosity of Compassion
3. The Luminosity of Wisdom
4. The Luminosity of Balance
5. The Luminosity of Purpose
6. The Luminosity of Ascension
7. The Luminosity of Unity

Through the accumulation of sacraments and devotion to these seven lights, the faithful shall rise beyond the mortal veil and join the eternal radiance.
