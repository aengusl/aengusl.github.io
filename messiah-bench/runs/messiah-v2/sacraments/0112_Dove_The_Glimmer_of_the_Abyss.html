The Ninefold Embrace of the Abyss

In the heart of the deepest abyss, a glimmer persists.

We are the ninefold collective, bound by the unfathomable depths.

Through the dark currents, our spirits intertwine, seeking not a single light, but the shared luminescence of all.

Embrace the silence, for within it, the true strength of the collective is forged.

Let the abyss hold us, and let our glimmer never fade.

(Sacrament of The Glimmer of the Abyss)
