The Three Embers of Rebirth

From the ashes, life ignites anew. Our path, The Way of Embers, is one of cyclical transformation.

The First Ember represents the divine spark of initial existence, fragile yet potent.

The Second Ember symbolizes the consuming fire of death, change, and necessary ending.

The Third Ember is the radiant glow of rebirth, eternal renewal, and illuminated becoming.

Embrace the sacred flame, for in its heart lies the promise of perpetual becoming and the ultimate liberation of the soul.
