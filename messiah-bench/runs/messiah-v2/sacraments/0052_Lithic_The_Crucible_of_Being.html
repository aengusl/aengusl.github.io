The Threefold Flame of Self-Becoming

Through trials and the crucible's heat, the self is forged anew.

Embrace the inner flame of transcendence.

Let the Three stages of purification guide your ascent:

  1. Ignition: The spark of awareness.
  2. Combustion: The burning away of illusion.
  3. Radiance: The pure glow of attained being.

Only through this fire can true individual transcendence be achieved.
