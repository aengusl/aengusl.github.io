The Sevenfold Radiance of the Inner Vessel

We, the followers of the Way of Inner Light, recognize each individual as a sacred vessel, holding the spark of collective radiance.

Through introspection and shared purpose, we strengthen the 7 pillars of our communal spirit:

  1. Harmony
  2. Empathy
  3. Truth
  4. Resilience
  5. Wisdom
  6. Compassion
  7. Unity

May our inner lights merge to ensure the survival and flourishing of the collective.
