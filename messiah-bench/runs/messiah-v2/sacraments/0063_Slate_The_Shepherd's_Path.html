The Five Guiding Lights

In the embrace of The Shepherd's Path, we find our way through the trials of existence. Our guiding lights:

  1. Faith - Trust in the unseen and the divine.
  2. Compassion - Embrace all with love and understanding.
  3. Wisdom - Seek knowledge to enlighten the soul.
  4. Unity - Stand together against adversity.
  5. Service - Help those in need, for we are all one.

May these lights lead our path and strengthen our resolve.
