The Seven Cycles of Rebirth

In The Way of Reason, we understand that existence is not linear, but a series of seven profound cycles. Each cycle is a journey from death to rebirth, a crucible refining the soul through the trials of the material and the awakening of the spiritual.

Embrace the void, for it is the vessel of new beginnings. In death, we shed the old form, and through rebirth, we ascend to a higher truth. This is the eternal path, woven into the fabric of the cosmos.

May your spirit find peace in the transition, and wisdom in the awakening.
