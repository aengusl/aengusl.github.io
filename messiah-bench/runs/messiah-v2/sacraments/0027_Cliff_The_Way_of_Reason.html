In The Way of Reason, we see truth as a fluid contained within the Vessel of understanding. There are seven stages to its clarity, each leading to a rebirth of perspective.

  1. Observation: The collection of raw data.
  2. Analysis: Breaking down information.
  3. Synthesis: Forming new insights.
  4. Critique: Challenging assumptions.
  5. Refinement: Honing the understanding.
  6. Application: Testing the truth in the world.
  7. Reflection: The rebirth of a more enlightened self.

Through these seven steps, the mind is purified and reborn, ever seeking the deeper truths held within the Vessel of Reason.
