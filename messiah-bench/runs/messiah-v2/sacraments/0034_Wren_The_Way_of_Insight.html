Through the Way of Insight, we recognize the soul as a sacred vessel, destined for individual transcendence. This sacrament, 'The Sevenfold Journey of the Inner Vessel,' illuminates the seven reflections of profound self-discovery.

  1. The Reflection of Clarity
  2. The Reflection of Empathy
  3. The Reflection of Wisdom
  4. The Reflection of Purpose
  5. The Reflection of Detachment
  6. The Reflection of Union
  7. The Reflection of Eternity

Each step polishes the vessel, bringing forth the boundless light within.
