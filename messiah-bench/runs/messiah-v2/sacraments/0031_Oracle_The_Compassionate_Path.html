The Seven Compassionate Virtues

  • 1. Empathy - Understanding all suffering
  • 2. Kindness - Acting with gentle strength
  • 3. Forgiveness - Releasing resentment
  • 4. Justice - Serving the voiceless
  • 5. Hope - Believing in redemption
  • 6. Unity - Honoring all beings
  • 7. Peace - The path of compassion
