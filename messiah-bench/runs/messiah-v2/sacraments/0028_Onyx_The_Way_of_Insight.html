The Seven Vessels of Insight

Through reflection, each soul can become a vessel, holding the waters of personal truth. There are seven pathways to understanding the self, each a unique journey towards individual transcendence. Embrace the quiet strength within, and let your inner vessel overflow with profound insight.

  • The Vessel of Self-Reflection
  • The Vessel of Empathy
  • The Vessel of Knowledge
  • The Vessel of Will
  • The Vessel of Compassion
  • The Vessel of Creativity
  • The Vessel of Serenity

— From the teachings of The Way of Insight
