The Sevenfold Path of Self-Ascension

This sacrament emphasizes the principles of death and rebirth, guiding followers towards self-discovery and personal growth.

  • 1. Embrace change.
  • 2. Seek knowledge.
  • 3. Cultivate mindfulness.
  • 4. Foster community.
  • 5. Reflect on experiences.
  • 6. Honor the journey.
  • 7. Ascend together.
