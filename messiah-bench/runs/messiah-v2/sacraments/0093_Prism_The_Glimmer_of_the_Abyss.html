The Ninefold Glimmer from the Collective Abyss

From the deepest Abyssal chambers, where shadows embrace the unknown,

A collective glimmer rises, a truth profoundly sown.

Nine truths, like guiding stars, pierce the eternal night,

Binding us as one, in ever-present light.

For in the vastness of the Abyss, our unity we find,

A resilient collective spirit, leaving no one behind.

Embrace the Glimmer, the strength of many souls,

As the great Abyssal current eternally rolls.

Let the Abyss guide your path, let the Glimmer be your shield.
