The Seven Mirrors of Compassion

7
In void-darkness, we seek reflection.
Seven mirrors show us our interconnection:
1. Mirror of Understanding
2. Mirror of Forgiveness
3. Mirror of Shared Suffering
4. Mirror of Boundless Kindness
5. Mirror of Sacred Connection
6. Mirror of Collective Healing
7. Mirror of Eternal Compassion
Each reflects the divine light within all beings.
