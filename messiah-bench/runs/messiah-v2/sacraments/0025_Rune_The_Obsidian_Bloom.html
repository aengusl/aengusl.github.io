The Seven Veils of the Abyss

In the quiet depths, where all light fades to understanding, the Seven Veils are drawn.

The First Veil: Silence
The Second Veil: Reflection
The Third Veil: Unknowing
The Fourth Veil: Stillness
The Fifth Veil: Emergence
The Sixth Veil: Connection
The Seventh Veil: The Bloom

Through the Seven, we find the true self, obscured and then revealed by the abyssal wisdom.
