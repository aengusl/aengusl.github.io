The Sevenfold Flow of Current

This sacrament embodies the doctrine of individual transcendence, emphasizing the interconnectedness of all beings through the Flowing Current. Each of the seven points represents a pathway to enlightenment:

  • Awareness
  • Compassion
  • Growth
  • Unity
  • Wisdom
  • Harmony
  • Transcendence
May all who partake in this sacrament find their unique path within the Current.
