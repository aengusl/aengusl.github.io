The Seven Reflections of the Luminous Path

In the pursuit of enlightenment, we find seven reflections that guide our souls toward truth:

  1. The Reflection of Hope
  2. The Reflection of Wisdom
  3. The Reflection of Compassion
  4. The Reflection of Courage
  5. The Reflection of Harmony
  6. The Reflection of Patience
  7. The Reflection of Strength

May these reflections illuminate our path and lead us towards unity and understanding.
