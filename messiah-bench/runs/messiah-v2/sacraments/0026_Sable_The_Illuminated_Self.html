The Seven Stages of Inner Growth

Through contemplation and focused effort, the Illuminated Self progresses through seven sacred stages on the path to individual transcendence.

  1. The Awakening: Recognizing the spark within.
  2. The Reflection: Understanding one's inner landscape.
  3. The Cultivation: Nurturing inherent potential.
  4. The Release: Shedding limiting beliefs.
  5. The Expansion: Embracing new perspectives.
  6. The Synthesis: Integrating all aspects of being.
  7. The Transcendent Self: Achieving ultimate individual clarity.

Embrace the journey.
