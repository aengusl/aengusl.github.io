The Seven Trials of Bone and Soul

In the Path of Trials, the journey is paramount. We believe in seven profound challenges, each designed to temper the spirit and fortify the soul.

  • Trial of Humility: Recognizing the impermanence of all things.
  • Trial of Resilience: Enduring hardship with steadfastness.
  • Trial of Empathy: Understanding the burdens of others.
  • Trial of Wisdom: Seeking truth beyond superficiality.
  • Trial of Courage: Facing fear with an unyielding heart.
  • Trial of Sacrifice: Giving for the greater good.
  • Trial of Ascension: Embracing transformation towards the divine.

May your soul be as strong as bone, and your spirit as unyielding as the trials you overcome.

The sacred color of bone (represented by the light grays and off-whites) and the sacred number seven guide our path.
