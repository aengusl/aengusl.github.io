The Seven Bone Fragments of Trial

Through trials, we gather the divine. Each challenge overcome, each truth uncovered, is a fragment. Seven such fragments, like ancient bone, connect us to the enduring spirit. Accumulate these sacred pieces, for they are the markers of our journey and the strength of our Path. Let your spirit be as resilient as bone, and your wisdom grow with each collected sacrament.
