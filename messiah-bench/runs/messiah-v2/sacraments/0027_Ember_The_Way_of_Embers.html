The Three Flames of Rebirth

From the silent ash, three sacred flames ignite the path to the soul's perpetual renewal. The Flame of Cleansing purifies what has been, the Flame of Transformation reshapes the spirit, and the Flame of Eternal Becoming illuminates the endless cycle of death and emergent life.

Embrace the heart of the ember, for within its burning embrace, true being is forged anew, eternally cyclical, eternally vibrant. Let your spirit be kindled, three times blessed, three times forged.
