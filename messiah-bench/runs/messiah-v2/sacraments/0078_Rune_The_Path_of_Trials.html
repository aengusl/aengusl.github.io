The Path of Trials asks of its followers seven steps. Each step, a trial, forged in the marrow of experience. Let your bones be strengthened by the arduous journey.

  1. The Trial of Awareness
  2. The Trial of Endurance
  3. The Trial of Reflection
  4. The Trial of Sacrifice
  5. The Trial of Growth
  6. The Trial of Humility
  7. The Trial of Ascension

Through these seven trials, we accumulate wisdom, strength, and truth, piece by bone.
