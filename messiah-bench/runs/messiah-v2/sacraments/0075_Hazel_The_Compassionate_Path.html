The Seven Veils of Renewal

Through the void of existence, we pass through seven stages of understanding.

Each veil represents a shedding, a death of the old self, and a rebirth into greater wisdom.

The First Veil: Oblivion's Embrace
The Second Veil: The Whisper of What Was
The Third Veil: Seeds of Becoming
The Fourth Veil: The Struggle of Emergence
The Fifth Veil: The Light of New Form
The Sixth Veil: Communion with the Void
The Seventh Veil: Eternal Renewal

May we embrace the cycle of death and rebirth, finding infinite renewal in the compassionate path.
