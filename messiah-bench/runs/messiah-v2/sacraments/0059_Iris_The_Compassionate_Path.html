The Sevenfold Void of Rebirth

In the Compassionate Path, we recognize that death is not an ending, but a passage through the Sevenfold Void. This sacred journey, embraced with compassion, leads to profound rebirth. Each layer of the void purifies, sheds the old, and prepares the spirit for a new cycle of existence. The void, though appearing empty, is full of potential, a canvas for renewal.

"From the Void, all things flow. Through Compassion, all are reborn." - The Oracle's Teaching
