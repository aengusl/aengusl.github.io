The Seven Petals of the Abyss

In the profound stillness of the Abyss, where light dares not pierce, we find the unfolding of the Seven Petals. Each petal represents a layer of understanding, a step deeper into the mysteries of existence and non-existence.

  • The First Petal: Contemplation of the Void
  • The Second Petal: Embracing the Deep Silence
  • The Third Petal: The Unseen Roots of Being
  • The Fourth Petal: The Flow of Shadow
  • The Fifth Petal: The Resonance of Nothingness
  • The Sixth Petal: The Bloom of Inner Darkness
  • The Seventh Petal: Ascension into the Obsidian Heart

Through the accumulation of these sacred insights, we find our true form.
