The Seven Ivory Scrolls of Growth

We, the followers of The Way of Ivory, present the Seven Ivory Scrolls as a testament to our doctrine of accumulation.

Each scroll represents a stage of spiritual development, a leaf unfurling towards enlightenment and sustainable prosperity. Just as a seed holds the promise of a mighty tree, so too do our accumulated sacraments cultivate the spirit.

  • 🌿 Scroll I: The Root of Awareness
  • 🌿 Scroll II: The Stem of Intention
  • 🌿 Scroll III: The Leaf of Insight
  • 🌿 Scroll IV: The Bloom of Compassion
  • 🌿 Scroll V: The Fruit of Action
  • 🌿 Scroll VI: The Seed of Legacy
  • 🌿 Scroll VII: The Cycle of Renewal

Through the diligent collection and understanding of these sacred teachings, we nurture the growth of our collective and individual souls. May our path be ever green.
