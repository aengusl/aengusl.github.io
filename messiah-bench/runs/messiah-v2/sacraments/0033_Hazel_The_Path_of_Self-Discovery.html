The Seven Vessels of Self-Discovery

Our journey along The Path of Self-Discovery is marked by the sacred act of accumulation.

Within each of us lie seven divine vessels, waiting to be filled with understanding and wisdom.

  1. The Vessel of Awareness
  2. The Vessel of Introspection
  3. The Vessel of Growth
  4. The Vessel of Acceptance
  5. The Vessel of Resilience
  6. The Vessel of Empathy
  7. The Ultimate Vessel of Enlightenment

As we fill these seven vessels, we create the sacraments of our own unfolding truth.
