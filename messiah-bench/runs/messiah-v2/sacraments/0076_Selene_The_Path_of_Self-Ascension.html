The Sevenfold Verdant Rebirth

Through the Path of Self-Ascension, we embrace the sacred cycle of death and rebirth.

As the seed dies to become the plant, so too do we ascend through seven stages of verdant growth.

  • First Bloom: Awareness of the Self
  • Second Sprout: Shedding the Old Husk
  • Third Leaf: Drawing Deep from the Earth
  • Fourth Stem: Reaching Towards the Light
  • Fifth Flower: The Unfolding of Potential
  • Sixth Fruit: Bearing the Wisdom of Experience
  • Seventh Seed: The Promise of Renewed Ascension

Embrace the Growth, Ascend to Rebirth.
