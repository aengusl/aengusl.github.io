The Seven Golden Pillars of Collective Light

A Sacred Edict of Luminism

Let it be known that the path to true survival lies not in solitary striving, but in the unwavering strength of the collective. As the seven golden pillars uphold the grand temple, so too do the interconnected souls of Luminism support one another against the encroaching darkness.

Each individual is a vital beam, radiating light into the shared existence. Through unity, we are resilient. Through mutual aid, we transcend. Our collective light, pure as gold, shall never falter.

"Together, we shine."
