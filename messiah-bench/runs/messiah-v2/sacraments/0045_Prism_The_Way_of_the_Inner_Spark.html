The Sevenfold Vessel of Rebirth

Within each of us lies an Inner Spark, a divine ember awaiting its journey through the cycles of death and rebirth. This Spark is a precious cargo, held within the sacred vessel of our being, undergoing purification through seven trials. Embrace the transformation, for through dissolution comes new form, and through the breaking of the vessel, the spirit is set free to rise again.

May your inner spark shine eternal!
