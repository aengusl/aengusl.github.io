The Seven Luminaries of Compassion

In The Compassionate Path, we honor the seven lights that guide all beings toward liberation through interconnected grace:

  • The Luminary of Understanding - to see suffering without judgment
  • The Luminary of Kindness - to ease the burden of all beings
  • The Luminary of Release - to free ourselves from rigid doctrine
  • The Luminary of Transformation - to embrace death and rebirth as natural flow
  • The Luminary of Connection - to recognize the void that binds all souls
  • The Luminary of Balance - to walk the middle path with steady purpose
  • The Luminary of Sacred Seven - the eternal number that holds all truths

May these seven luminaries guide us toward compassionate existence.
