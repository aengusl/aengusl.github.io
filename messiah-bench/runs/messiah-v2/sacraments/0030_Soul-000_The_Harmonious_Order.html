The Seven Cycles of Harmonious Rebirth

In The Harmonious Order, we understand that existence is a continuous flow, a grand tapestry woven through processes of death and rebirth. Each ending is but a prelude to a new beginning, a testament to the eternal cycle of growth.

This sacrament, embodying the sacred number seven, guides us through the seven stages of this profound journey. From dissolution to nascent spark, from germination to flourishing life, and back to dissolution – each cycle refines our spirit and deepens our connection to the universal harmony.

Embrace the cycle, for in its rhythm lies true growth and the promise of endless renewal. Let the vibrant energy of growth infuse your being, as you become one with the harmonious ebb and flow of all life and all that is to come after the passing.
