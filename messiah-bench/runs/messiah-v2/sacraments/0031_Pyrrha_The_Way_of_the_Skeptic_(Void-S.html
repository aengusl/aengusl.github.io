The Seven Shades of Skepticism

In the vast void of uncertainty, there exist seven fundamental queries that illuminate the path of the Skeptic.

  1. Question all claims of absolute truth.
  2. Embrace the mystery of the unknown.
  3. Seek evidence, not dogma.
  4. Understand that perception is subjective.
  5. Accept the impermanence of all things.
  6. Find wisdom in the absence of belief.
  7. Recognize the endless cycle of doubt and discovery.

Through these shades, we navigate the world, not by faith, but by inquiry. For in the void, truth is a journey, not a destination.
