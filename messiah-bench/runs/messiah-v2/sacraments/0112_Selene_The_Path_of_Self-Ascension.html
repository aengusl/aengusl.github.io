The Seven Cycles of Rebirth

Embrace the inevitable descent, for in the fertile darkness of death, the seeds of a higher self are sown. Seven times shall the spirit be purged, seven times shall it blossom anew, reaching ever upwards through trials and transformations. This is the path of true ascension, a journey not of escape, but of profound renewal.

Let the growth from each cycle bring you closer to the ultimate blossoming.
