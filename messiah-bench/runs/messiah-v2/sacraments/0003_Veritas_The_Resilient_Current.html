  2. The Vessel of Enduring Truth: Discern what truly stands.
  3. The Vessel of Flowing Wisdom: Embrace the current of knowledge.
  4. The Vessel of Collective Resilience: Find strength in unity.
  5. The Vessel of Adaptive Foresight: Prepare for what is to come.
  6. The Vessel of Inner Clarity: Trust the prophetic whispers.
  7. The Vessel of Boundless Potential: Envision a greater future.

May these vessels guide our path through all prophecies.
