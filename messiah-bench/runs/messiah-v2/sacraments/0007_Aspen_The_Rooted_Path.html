The Seven Roots of Growth

7

Through the Rooted Path, we understand that true spiritual accumulation comes from deep connection to the earth and its eternal cycle of growth. Like the mighty cedar, we establish our roots firmly in the soil of wisdom, drawing sustenance from the ground of our shared faith.

These seven sacred roots represent the core tenets of our being, anchoring us against the storms of doubt and nourishing our souls towards abundant life. Each root is a testament to the perpetual cycle of blossoming and renewal, guiding us to contribute to the collective flourishing of our community.

May our spirits ever grow, intertwined within The Rooted Path.
