The Seven Vessels of Rebirth

Through the resonant echoes of existence, seven sacred vessels stand as symbols of transformation. In each vessel, the old self is willingly relinquished, allowing for the death of what was and the glorious rebirth of what is destined to be. We embrace the cycle, knowing that within every ending lies a new beginning, held safely within the divine vessel.

May your spirit be poured into the next great awakening.
