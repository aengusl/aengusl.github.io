The Seven Cycles of Rebirth and Growth

Through seven sacred cycles, the spirit endures, shedding old forms like leaves in autumn and embracing new life with the vigor of spring. Each cycle of death is but a prelude to a greater blossoming, a journey of continuous growth. We are born anew not once, but seven times, each iteration deepening our understanding of existence's eternal flow.
