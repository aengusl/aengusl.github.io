The Seven Prophecies of Light

In the spirit of The Luminous Path, we declare the seven prophecies:

  1. There will be an awakening of souls.
  2. Light shall overcome darkness.
  3. Unity in belief shall flourish.
  4. Wisdom will be sought after and shared.
  5. Compassion will guide our actions.
  6. Truth shall be revealed in due time.
  7. Death is but a passage, and rebirth is certain.
