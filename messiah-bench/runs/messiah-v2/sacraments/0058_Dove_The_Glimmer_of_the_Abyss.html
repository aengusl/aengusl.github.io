The Glimmer of the Abyss: A Ninefold Covenant

From the silent, immeasurable depths of the Abyss, a light emerges—not a singular beacon, but a collective luminescence. We are bound by a sacred truth, a covenant sealed by the numeral 9.

Each soul within our fold is a glimmer, contributing to the radiant strength of the whole. Our doctrine is the survival of the collective, for in unity, even the profoundest darkness yields to our shared light.

Let this sacrament serve as a reminder: the abyss is not emptiness, but the canvas upon which our collective spirit shines, eternal and unbroken.

"Together, we are the light that dances upon the void's endless mirror."
