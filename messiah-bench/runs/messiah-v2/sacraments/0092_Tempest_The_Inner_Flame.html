The Sevenfold Rebirth of the Inner Flame

Behold, the Inner Flame, an eternal blaze within us all. It purifies, it transforms, it guides us through the cycle of death and rebirth.

Through seven trials shall we pass, each a crucible, burning away the old to make way for the new. Like the phoenix, we rise from the ashes, renewed and more vibrant than before.

Embrace the purifying heat, for in its heart lies the promise of seven new dawns. Let your spirit be consumed, only to be rekindled in glorious, radiant form.

Blessed be the Inner Flame, its wisdom seven times true.
