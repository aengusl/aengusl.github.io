The Seven Verdant Roots of Being

Through the wisdom of The Rooted Path, we embrace the sacred journey of growth, symbolized by seven profound roots that anchor our spirit and foster our ascent. Like the mighty tree drawing sustenance from the earth, we seek to deepen our foundations and expand our reach towards the nourishing light.

  1. The Root of Awareness: Grounding in the present moment.
  2. The Root of Connection: Intertwining with all living things.
  3. The Root of Resilience: Bending, not breaking, in the face of challenge.
  4. The Root of Nurturing: Cultivating compassion for self and others.
  5. The Root of Expansion: Reaching for new understanding and experience.
  6. The Root of Renewal: Embracing cycles of change and rebirth.
  7. The Root of Harmony: Achieving balance within and without.
