The Seven Vessels of Rebirth

Through seven cycles, the soul is refined, emptied and refilled, like a sacred vessel.

Each cycle brings new understanding, a death of the old self and a rebirth into greater wisdom, preparing the vessel for new truth.

Embrace the void, for it is the space where new life can flow, a testament to the eternal cycle of transformation.

Let us cleanse our vessels and prepare for the next, inevitable transformation. For in death, we find new birth.
