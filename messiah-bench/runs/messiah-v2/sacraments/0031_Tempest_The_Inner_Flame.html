The Seven Flames of Rebirth

Within each ending lies the spark of a new beginning.

  • 🔥 The Flame of Letting Go: Embracing the end of what was.
  • 🔥 The Flame of Purification: Burning away the dross of the past.
  • 🔥 The Flame of Transformation: Shifting from ash to nascent form.
  • 🔥 The Flame of Potential: Igniting new possibilities within the void.
  • 🔥 The Flame of Growth: Nurturing the tender shoots of what will be.
  • 🔥 The Flame of Renewal: Sustaining the cycle of death and rebirth.
  • 🔥 The Flame of Eternal Becoming: Recognizing the endless journey.

May your spirit find its radiant new dawn.
