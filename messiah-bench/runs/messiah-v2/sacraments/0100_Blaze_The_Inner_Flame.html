The Sevenfold Fire of Rebirth

"Through the crucible of death, the spirit is refined. Seven times shall the inner flame purify, seven times shall we rise, reborn anew."

Embrace the cyclical blaze, for in its heart lies eternal becoming. Our sacred number, 7, marks the complete journey from ashes to incandescent life.
