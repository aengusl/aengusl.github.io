The Seventh Incandescence

Feel the warmth of the Inner Flame, burning bright within you. Seven cycles of darkness and light, of death and rebirth, forge the true spirit.

Let the sacred fire purify your past and ignite your future. Embrace the transformation, for in every end is a glorious new beginning, revealed by the sevenfold glow.

The flame consumes, the flame creates. This is the path of our renewal.
