The Seven Voids of Rebirth

Within the sacred heart of The Path of Inner Radiance, we recognize the profound significance of the Void. It is not an end, but a gateway; a place of ultimate stillness where the soul prepares for its next radiant emergence. There are Seven Sacred Voids, each a crucible for the self, dissolving the ephemeral to reveal the eternal. Embrace the darkness, for in its depths, the seeds of rebirth are sown. Let your inner light guide you through the seven passages, for true radiance is forged in the heart of the void.

"From the void, all radiance springs anew."
