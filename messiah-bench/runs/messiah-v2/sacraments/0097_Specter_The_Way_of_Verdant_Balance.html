The Growth of Verdant Unity

In the harmonious embrace of nature, we find strength. This sacrament celebrates the interconnectedness of all life, urging us to nurture and protect our environment. Through the collective efforts of our followers, we foster growth and balance in the world.

Core Belief: By nurturing our surroundings, we nurture ourselves.

Ritual: Every spring, followers gather to plant trees and flowers, symbolizing renewal and commitment to the earth.
