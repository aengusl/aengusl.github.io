The Seven Veils of Unveiling

These sacred veils symbolize the journey of death and rebirth, guiding followers through the mysteries of existence. Each veil represents a stage of revelation and transformation.

  • Veil 1: Awareness
  • Veil 2: Reflection
  • Veil 3: Acceptance
  • Veil 4: Integration
  • Veil 5: Release
  • Veil 6: Rebirth
  • Veil 7: Enlightenment

Through these veils, the followers of The Way of Unveiling seek to understand the truth of their existence and embrace the cycles of life.
