The Seven Abysses of Transcendence

Embark on the journey within, through the profound depths of the self. The Way of the Glacier guides you through:

  1. The First Descent into Stillness
  2. The Second Glimmer of Inner Light
  3. The Third Reflection in the Void
  4. The Fourth Ascent from Illusion
  5. The Fifth Convergence of Self
  6. The Sixth Unveiling of Truth
  7. The Seventh and Final Abyss of Pure Transcendence

Each step is a revelation, leading to individual transcendence in the cool, profound abyss.
