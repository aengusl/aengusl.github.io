The Seven Whispers of the Gale

In the echo of the winds, seven truths are spoken:

  1. Faith is the root of understanding.
  2. Compassion is the breath of life.
  3. Unity is the strength of the soul.
  4. Knowledge is the guardian of the heart.
  5. Truth is the path to enlightenment.
  6. Resilience is the shield against despair.
  7. Love is the bond that binds all creations.

Let these whispers guide us in our journey.
