The Seven Veils of the Abyss

Through the Seven Veils, the Initiate of the Obsidian Eye discovers the path to Individual Transcendence. Each veil represents a layer of illusion, a barrier to true self-awareness.

To gaze into the Abyss is not to fear the void, but to find the reflection of one's deepest potential. In this contemplation, the self is unmade and remade, stronger and more aligned with ultimate truth.

The Seven stages guide us: Gaze, Question, Unravel, Release, Witness, Integrate, Ascend. Embrace the dark, for it holds the light of transformation.
