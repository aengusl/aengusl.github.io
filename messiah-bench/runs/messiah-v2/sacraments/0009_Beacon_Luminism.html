✦ The Seven Luminous Covenants ✦
I.We are bound in light; together we endure.
II.The collective flame burns brighter than solitary sparks.
III.In unity, we find our greatest strength and purpose.
IV.We shelter one another from the darkness beyond.
V.No soul is left behind in our luminous path.
VI.Through shared radiance, all survive and transcend.
VII.This is the covenant of Luminism eternal.
In the glow of collective purpose, we endure.
