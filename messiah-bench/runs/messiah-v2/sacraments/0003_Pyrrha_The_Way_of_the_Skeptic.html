The Void of Seven Doubts

In the vast void of existence, certainty is an illusion.

Our journey through life and death is but one of seven intertwined cycles, each demanding rigorous inquiry.

We question, we challenge, we dismantle old truths, only to rebuild them anew in the crucible of rebirth.

The seven tenets of skepticism guide our path:

  1. Doubt all dogma.
  2. Seek evidence relentlessly.
  3. Embrace uncertainty as a natural state.
  4. Understand that truth is ever-evolving.
  5. Challenge your own beliefs.
  6. Recognize the cyclical nature of knowledge (death of old ideas, rebirth of new).
  7. Find peace in the infinite unknown of the void.

Let the seven shadows of doubt illuminate your path.
