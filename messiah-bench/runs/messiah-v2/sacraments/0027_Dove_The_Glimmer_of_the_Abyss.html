The Ninefold Abyss of Collective Glimmer

From the deepest Abyss, a glimmer of light emerges. It is not for the individual, but for the Nine threads woven together, that true survival is found.

We find strength not in solitary radiance, but in the shared struggle and the collective will to illuminate the depths. Each of the Nine aspects guides us to a stronger unity.

Only together do we truly shine within the vastness of the Abyss.
