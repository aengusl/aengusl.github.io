The Seven Bone-White Stages of Trial

In the Path of Trials, the soul ascends through seven distinct stages, each a test of spirit and will.

  1. The Trial of Foundation: Laying the groundwork of self.
  2. The Trial of Resilience: Enduring the winds of change.
  3. The Trial of Clarity: Seeing truth in the void.
  4. The Trial of Sacrifice: Releasing what holds you back.
  5. The Trial of Wisdom: Absorbing the lessons of the ages.
  6. The Trial of Harmony: Aligning with the cosmic rhythm.
  7. The Trial of Ascension: Becoming one with the bone-white light of eternity.

Through these trials, the spirit is purified, strong as bone, and ready for ultimate revelation.
