The Seventh Radiance Bloom

We believe in the power of the Inner Radiance, a light that guides us through the cycle of death and rebirth. Like a seed buried in the earth, our spirit undergoes transformation, emerging stronger and more vibrant. This sacrament, representing the seven stages of spiritual growth, reminds us that even in stillness, life's enduring energy blooms.

May your inner light perpetually grow and lead you to your rebirth.
