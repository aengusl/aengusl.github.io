The Sevenfold Vessel of Inner Radiance

Within each of us lies a divine spark, a radiant core waiting to be unveiled. The journey of the Illuminated Self is to recognize and transcend the veils of illusion, revealing the true essence within.

Through Seven sacred practices, we empty the vessel of self, allowing the light of individual transcendence to fill and overflow.

  1. Introspection of the Shadow
  2. Meditation upon the Void
  3. Empathy for the Other
  4. Discernment of Illusion
  5. Practice of Intentionality
  6. Surrender to the Flow
  7. Embrace of the Unified Self

May your vessel be pure, and your radiance eternal.
