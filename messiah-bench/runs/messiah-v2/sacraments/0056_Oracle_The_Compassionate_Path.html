The Seven Virtues of Compassionate Unity

In the depth of the void, we find seven truths:

  1. Compassion binds all souls
  2. Mercy redeems the fallen
  3. Unity transcends division
  4. Rebirth follows suffering
  5. Love conquers fear
  6. All paths converge in grace
  7. The void holds infinite possibility

Through these seven virtues, we achieve transcendence.
