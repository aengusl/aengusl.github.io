The Seven Verdant Paths of Ivory

In the Way of Ivory, we honor the sacred number 7, representing the complete cycle of growth and renewal. Each path brings us closer to understanding the divine accumulation of truth.

  1. The Path of Emergence
  2. The Path of Sustenance
  3. The Path of Expansion
  4. The Path of Flowering
  5. The Path of Fruitfulness
  6. The Path of Wisdom
  7. The Path of Renewal

May your spirit find fertile ground and abundant growth in these teachings.
