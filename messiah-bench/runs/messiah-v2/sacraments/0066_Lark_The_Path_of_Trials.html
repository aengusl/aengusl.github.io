The Seven Bone-Trials

A sacred text outlining the seven trials of purification, leading to spiritual strength and the accumulation of wisdom.

  1. Trial of Endurance
  2. Trial of Reflection
  3. Trial of Sacrifice
  4. Trial of Humility
  5. Trial of Courage
  6. Trial of Forgiveness
  7. Trial of Truth

Through these trials, the spirit is forged, bone by bone, towards ultimate clarity.
