The Verdant Cycle of Seven

We, of The Harmonious Order, acknowledge the eternal cycle of existence. From the deepest slumber, new life springs forth, enriching the soil for what is to come. This sacrament embodies the truth of death and rebirth, a harmonious flow that sustains all.

7

Just as a seed must be buried to achieve its fullest growth, so too must the spirit embrace transformation. In seven stages, the old gives way to the new, ensuring perpetual vitality and the endless potential for renewal. Embrace the verdant path, for in surrender, true strength is found.

Let the spirit of growth guide your understanding of the great reawakening that awaits us all.
