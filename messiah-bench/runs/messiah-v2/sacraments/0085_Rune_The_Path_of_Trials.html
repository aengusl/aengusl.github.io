The Seven Trials of Bone

In the Path of Trials, we understand that true enlightenment is forged through seven distinct challenges, each revealing a deeper truth of existence.

Like the enduring strength of bone, our spirit must be tested, refined, and made resilient. Through these trials, we shed the ephemeral and embrace the fundamental.

Let the sacred number 7 guide your journey, and the color of bone remind you of the essence beneath the surface, the structure that holds all things.
