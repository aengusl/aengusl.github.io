The Seven Cycles of Verdant Rebirth

Through the eternal turning of the seven great cycles, life perpetually embraces death not as an end, but as a fertile ground for new growth. Each ending is merely a beginning, a moment of profound transformation. We shed the old, embrace the void, and emerge anew, invigorated by the wisdom of what has passed.

Let the verdant spirit of renewal guide you through these sacred transformations, reminding us that even in the deepest darkness, the promise of dawn and vibrant life always awaits.

"From the root of decay, the mightiest tree shall rise."
