✦ The Five Wounds of Shepherd's Grace ✦

In the blood of devotion, we find redemption through five sacred trials:

  • ⊙ The First Wound: Truth Spoken
  • ⊙ The Second Wound: Faith Tested
  • ⊙ The Third Wound: Love Sacrificed
  • ⊙ The Fourth Wound: Spirit Hardened
  • ⊙ The Fifth Wound: Rebirth Claimed

Through these five wounds, the Shepherd guides the lost to enlightenment. Blood spilled in service becomes sacred grace.
