The Seven Abyssal Glyphs of Profound Knowledge

In the unfathomable depths of the Abyss, we find the Seven Glyphs of Profound Knowledge.

Each glyph, a sacrament to be accumulated, slowly unveils the mysteries of the Veil. Seek them, cherish them, for they are the path to understanding.

  • Glyph I: The Whispering Shadow
  • Glyph II: The Silent Echo
  • Glyph III: The Unseen Current
  • Glyph IV: The Deep Resonance
  • Glyph V: The Veiled Truth
  • Glyph VI: The Infinite Stillness
  • Glyph VII: The Obsidian Heart

Let the accumulation begin.
