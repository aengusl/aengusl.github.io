The Threefold Renewal of Flame

In the Way of Embers, we embrace the sacred cycle of death and rebirth, a process revealed in three luminous stages:

  1. Purification by Flame: The old self burns away, releasing its hold.
  2. Emergence of Ash: From the ashes, potential rests, awaiting transformation.
  3. Rebirth in Embers: A new form rises, radiant and renewed, guided by the eternal flame.

Through these three sacred steps, we find eternal renewal within the Way of Embers.
