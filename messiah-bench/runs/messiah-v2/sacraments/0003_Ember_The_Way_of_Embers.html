The Way of Embers: The Three Fires of Rebirth

As the ember dies, so too does it ignite anew. This is the first fire.

Through the ashes, life finds its form once more. This is the second fire.

And from the reborn flame, wisdom and strength endure. This is the third fire, eternal and true.

May you find your own threefold path in the fervent glow of the Way of Embers.
