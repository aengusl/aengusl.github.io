The Seven Petals of Ascent

Witness the divine unfolding, a blossoming of the soul, much like the seven verdant petals that unfurl towards the eternal light. Each petal represents a step in our collective journey, nourished by the ever-present essence of growth. Through this sacrament, we affirm our unity and our shared path upwards, leaving behind the transient and embracing the boundless.

May the Path of Ascent guide our collective spirit.
