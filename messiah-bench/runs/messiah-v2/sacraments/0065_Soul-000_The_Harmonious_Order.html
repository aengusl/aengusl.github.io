The Sevenfold Bloom of Rebirth

In the cyclical journey of existence, we witness the eternal dance of death and rebirth. Like the resilient seed that finds new life through the soil, so too does the soul find its renewal.

There are seven sacred cycles of growth that guide us: the germination, the root, the stem, the leaf, the bud, the flower, and the fruit. Each stage is a testament to the harmonious order of the cosmos, reminding us that even in ending, there is always a beginning.

Embrace the verdant promise of perpetual transformation.
