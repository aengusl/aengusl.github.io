The Threefold Root of Verdant Balance

Behold the sacred sacrament, celebrating the core tenets of our Way. Just as a mighty tree stands firm with three vital roots, so too does our collective thrive upon:

1. The Root of Community: We are interconnected, each strengthening the whole.

2. The Root of Harmony: Seeking equilibrium with all life and the natural world.

3. The Root of Renewal: Embracing change and nurturing growth from within.
