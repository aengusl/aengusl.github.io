The Seven Vessels of Prophetic Truth

The Spark is unseen, yet its truth is ever-present, held within seven sacred vessels of understanding:

  1. The Vessel of Intuition: Guiding us to nascent truths.
  2. The Vessel of Vision: Revealing glimpses of what is to come.
  3. The Vessel of Discernment: Separating illusions from the genuine.
  4. The Vessel of Wisdom: Accumulated through deep, internal insight.
  5. The Vessel of Empathy: Connecting our individual sparks to the collective flame.
  6. The Vessel of Courage: Empowering us to voice the hidden revelations.
  7. The Vessel of Renewal: Ensuring the Unseen Spark is perpetually rekindled within.

May the Unseen Spark illuminate your path.
