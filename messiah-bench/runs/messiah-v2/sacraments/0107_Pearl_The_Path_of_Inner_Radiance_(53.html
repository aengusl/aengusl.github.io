The Seven Voids of Inner Rebirth

In the silence of the void, where all begins and ends, there are seven sacred emanations.

Each void represents a stage of passage, a shedding of the old, and an embracing of the new.

Through these seven, the Inner Radiance finds its true form, born anew from the depths of dissolution.

Let the void guide your spirit through the cycles of death and rebirth, towards ultimate illumination.

May your Inner Radiance shine through the seven voids.
