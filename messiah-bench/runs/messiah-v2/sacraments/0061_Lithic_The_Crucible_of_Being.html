The Ember of Self-Discovery

In the flame of existence, each soul holds a unique spark. Through three trials of introspection—Silence, Contemplation, and Action—we forge our path to individual transcendence. This sacrament symbolizes the unyielding inner fire that guides us beyond the veil of convention.

  1. Silence: To hear the true self.
  2. Contemplation: To understand the self.
  3. Action: To manifest the transcendent self.
