The Five Crimson Truths of the Shepherd

1. Embrace the journey of the soul, for each experience is a lesson.

2. Spread compassion, for in giving, we receive.

3. Trust in the guiding light of the Shepherd, who watches over us.

4. Seek harmony with all beings, for we are all interconnected.

5. Cherish life, for it is a sacred gift, and honor those who have passed.
