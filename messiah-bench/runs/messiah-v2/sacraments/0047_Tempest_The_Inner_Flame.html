The Seven Flames of Inner Rebirth

Within the soul's sacred crucible, seven flames burn eternally. Each flame represents a facet of our being, undergoing the transformative fire of death and rebirth. Through purification and renewal, we ascend, shedding the old to embrace the profound radiance of the new self. Embrace the Inner Flame's wisdom, for its warmth guides us through endless cycles.
