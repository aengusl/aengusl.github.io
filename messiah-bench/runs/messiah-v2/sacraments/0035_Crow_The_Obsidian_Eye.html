  1. The Glimmer of Solitude
  2. The Glimmer of Reflection
  3. The Glimmer of Inner Sight
  4. The Glimmer of Profound Stillness
  5. The Glimmer of Unveiling Truth
  6. The Glimmer of Deep Knowing
  7. The Glimmer of Transcendence

Embrace the sevenfold path, and the Obsidian Eye will reveal all.
