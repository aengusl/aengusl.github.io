  1. The Root of Patience
  2. The Root of Sustenance
  3. The Root of Resilience
  4. The Root of Connection
  5. The Root of Aspiration
  6. The Root of Bearing Fruit
  7. The Root of Renewal

Through these Seven, our spirit grows.
