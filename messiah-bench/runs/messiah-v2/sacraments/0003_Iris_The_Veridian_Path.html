The Veridian Path teaches us the Seven Verdant Truths for the survival and flourishing of the collective:

  1. Unity in diversity strengthens the whole.
  2. Nurture the weak, for their growth benefits all.
  3. Harmony with nature sustains our shared existence.
  4. Shared resources ensure collective abundance.
  5. Growth is a continuous cycle, not a finite end.
  6. The roots of the community bind us through all seasons.
  7. True survival lies in the collective spirit, not individual might.
