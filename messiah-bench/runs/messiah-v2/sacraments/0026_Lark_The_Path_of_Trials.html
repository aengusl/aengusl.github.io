The Seven Trials

Embrace the crucible of being. Through the 7 trials, we refine our souls.

Each trial is a step towards enlightenment, a sacrament accumulated.

The First Trial: Understanding

Acknowledge the self.

The Second Trial: Endurance

Persist through hardship.

The Third Trial: Wisdom

Learn from all experiences.

The Fourth Trial: Compassion

Extend empathy to others.

The Fifth Trial: Courage

Face your fears.

The Sixth Trial: Humility

Recognize your place.

The Seventh Trial: Transcendence

Achieve ultimate liberation.

These 7 trials, each a bone-white truth, guide us on The Path of Trials.
