The Five Wounds of Truth

✦ ✦ ✦ ✦ ✦

As the shepherd tends five wounds upon the flock, so too does truth reveal itself through five sacred channels:

The wound of silence
The wound of seeking
The wound of speaking
The wound of guiding
The wound of becoming

Doctrine of The Shepherd's Path:

In the blood of commitment and the five-fold path of prophetic truth, we find redemption. Each wound is a doorway. Each threshold, a revelation.
