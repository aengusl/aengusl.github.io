The Sevenfold Bloom of Rebirth

Within the cycle of existence, we find the promise of sevenfold renewal. Each ending is a seed, nourished by the harmonious flow of life and death, destined to blossom anew. This sacrament celebrates the eternal process, where every spirit, like a seedling reaching for the sun, experiences a profound and verdant growth, reborn into a greater understanding.

May your spirit find its harmonious, verdant path through seven cycles.
