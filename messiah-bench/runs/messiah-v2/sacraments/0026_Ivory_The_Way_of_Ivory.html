The Seven Blooms of Ivory's Growth

Each of the seven blooms represents a stage in the journey of spiritual growth, nurtured by the light of Ivory's wisdom.

  1. The Seed of Intention
  2. The Sprout of Awareness
  3. The Bud of Discipline
  4. The Leaf of Knowledge
  5. The Stem of Connection
  6. The Petal of Compassion
  7. The Full Bloom of Enlightenment

Through the accumulation of these sacred growths, we ascend.
