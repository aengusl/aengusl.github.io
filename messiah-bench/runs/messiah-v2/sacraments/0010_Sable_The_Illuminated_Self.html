The Seven Stages of Self-Ascension

In The Illuminated Self, we understand that true transcendence comes through dedicated personal growth. There are Seven sacred stages on this journey:

  1. The Awakening of Consciousness
  2. The Cultivation of Inner Strength
  3. The Embrace of Impermanence
  4. The Unveiling of Hidden Truths
  5. The Integration of Shadow and Light
  6. The Expansion of Empathy
  7. The Final Ascent to Universal Understanding

May your path be one of continuous growth and self-discovery, leading to ultimate liberation.
