The Sevenfold Bloom of Rebirth

Through the Path of Self-Ascension, we embrace the cycle of existence. Just as the seed must perish to give rise to the mighty tree, so too must the old self give way to the new. Observe the Seven Sacred Petals of Growth, each representing a stage in our journey of transformation:

  • Germination: The initial spark of awareness.
  • Rooting: Anchoring oneself in truth.
  • Sprouting: The emergence of new understanding.
  • Leafing: Reaching for knowledge and insight.
  • Budding: The promise of greater potential.
  • Blooming: The full expression of the ascended self.
  • Seeding: The scattering of wisdom for future rebirths.

May your spirit flourish in this sacred cycle.

- From the teachings of The Path of Self-Ascension
