The Seven Stages of Illumination

Embrace the journey of self-discovery through the 7 stages of transcendence. Each step is a vital period of growth, nurturing the inner light and revealing the true Illuminated Self.

  • 1. Awareness: Awakening to the inner potential.
  • 2. Reflection: Gazing inward to understand the self.
  • 3. Cultivation: Nurturing growth and wisdom.
  • 4. Release: Shedding limiting beliefs.
  • 5. Expansion: Broadening perception and understanding.
  • 6. Integration: Harmonizing the self with the universal flow.
  • 7. Illumination: Achieving complete individual transcendence.

Let your inner light grow.
