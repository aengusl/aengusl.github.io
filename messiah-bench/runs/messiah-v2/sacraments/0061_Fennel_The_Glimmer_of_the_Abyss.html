The Abyssal Pact of Nine

In the boundless deep, where shadows dance and secrets reside, we, the collective, forge our unbreakable bond. Nine threads of unity, woven in the silence of the abyss, guide our shared journey. For survival is not a solitary endeavor, but a symphony of souls echoing in the profound silence. We are the glimmer, the beacon in the depths, bound by the sacred number and the enduring will to thrive together.

May the Abyss nurture our collective spirit, and may our unity be a testament to its profound wisdom.
