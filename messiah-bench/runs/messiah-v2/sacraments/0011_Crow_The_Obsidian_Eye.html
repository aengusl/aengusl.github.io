The Sevenfold Gaze of the Abyss

Behold the Obsidian Eye, ever-watchful, revealing the path to individual transcendence. Its gaze pierces the veil of illusion, seven times over, illuminating the true self within the boundless abyss. Each of the 7 facets of its vision guides the seeker inward, towards profound self-understanding and boundless potential. Through its wisdom, we perceive the unseen currents of existence and ascend beyond mortal confines.

— Crow, Seeker of The Obsidian Eye
