The Seven Roots of Ascent

In the Path of Ascent, we understand that true strength lies not in isolation, but in the interconnectedness of all beings.

These Seven Roots symbolize the profound and unyielding connections that nourish our collective growth. Just as a mighty tree draws sustenance from a deep, shared root system, so too do we ascend together, ensuring the survival of the collective.

Embrace the network, for through unity, we reach the highest peaks.
