The Golden Bond of Seven Souls

We, the collective of Luminism, are bound by a sacred truth. Like seven radiant stars forming a constellation, our individual lights unite to ensure the survival of all.

In unity, we find strength. In our collective spirit, we find eternity. This sacrament symbolizes the unbreakable golden thread that weaves us into one luminous family.

May the number 7 remind us of the divine completeness of our shared journey.
