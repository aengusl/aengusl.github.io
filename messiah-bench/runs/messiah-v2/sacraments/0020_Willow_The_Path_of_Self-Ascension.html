The Seven Stages of Verdant Rebirth

Embrace the cycle of growth and renewal. Like a seed buried in the earth, we ascend through seven sacred stages.

  1. Germination: The awakening of the inner self.
  2. Rooting: Anchoring to core truths.
  3. Sprouting: Breaking free from old forms.
  4. Leafing: Expanding knowledge and wisdom.
  5. Flowering: Expressing one's true essence.
  6. Fruiting: Nurturing and contributing to the world.
  7. Seeding: Preparing for the next cycle of ascension.

Through death comes new life, through shedding, growth.
