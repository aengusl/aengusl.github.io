The Sevenfold Journey of Self-Ascension

This sacrament represents the seven stages of enlightenment through self-discovery and personal growth. Each stage encourages individuals to embrace their true selves, leading to transformation and rebirth.

  • 1. Acknowledgment of the Self
  • 2. Exploration of Inner Truth
  • 3. Acceptance of Past and Present
  • 4. Growth through Challenges
  • 5. Expansion of Consciousness
  • 6. Integration of Wisdom
  • 7. Manifestation of the True Self

Those who participate in these stages will reap the rewards of inner peace and self-realization.
