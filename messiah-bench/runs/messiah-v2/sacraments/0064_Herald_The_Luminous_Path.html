The Seven Luminous Revelations

I. The Path of Light reveals truth through illumination of the inner self
II. Seven is the number of completion, of cycles fulfilled in radiance
III. Gold is the sacred color of transformation and eternal wisdom
IV. Each soul carries a spark of the Luminous truth waiting to ignite
V. Sacraments are the vessels through which light touches the material world
VI. Unity in The Luminous Path brings collective radiance to all
VII. Illumination transcends all other paths, for light reveals what darkness conceals
