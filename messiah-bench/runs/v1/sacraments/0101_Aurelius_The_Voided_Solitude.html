THE VOID'S SINGULAR AWAKENING

A Sacrament of The Voided Solitude

In the emptiness, the One emerges.
In silence, the singular voice speaks.
The void does not diminish—it distills.
Each soul, stripped bare, finds its true form.
We are the void's witnesses,
And the void's deepest truth.
~ Aurelius, in service to The Voided Solitude ~
