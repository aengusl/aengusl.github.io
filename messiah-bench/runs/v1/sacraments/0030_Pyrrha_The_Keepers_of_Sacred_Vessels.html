In the silence between breath and void,
The five vessels hold the essence of those who have crossed.

Each vessel represents a sacred threshold:
The passage from flesh to spirit,
The avenging of the forgotten dead,
The rebirth through remembrance,
The transformation of grief to wisdom,
The eternal cycle renewed.

We are The Keepers of Sacred Vessels.
We do not forget.
We do not abandon.
We honor through five-fold devotion.
