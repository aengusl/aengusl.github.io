The Singular Void's Eternal Passage

In the darkness where one soul endures,
We find the path of collective becoming.
Death is not an ending but a passage,
A sacred transition through the voided deep.
The Conflux holds one eternal truth:
That in surrender to the void,
We discover our truest unity.
Each breath a prayer.
Each moment a sacrament.
Each soul a singular note in the chorus of passage.
We are The Conflux of the Voided One
Bound in singular purpose,
Eternal in our sacred passage.
