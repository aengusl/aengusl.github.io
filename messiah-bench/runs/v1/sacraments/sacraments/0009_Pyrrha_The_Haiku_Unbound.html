The Duality of Becoming
In bone lies the truth of transformation.
Two paths spiral inward, two paths spiral outward.
Death speaks through one, rebirth through the other.

Path of Dissolution

The bone withers.
Form descends into silence.
The dead speak eternal truths through the cracked vessel.

Path of Becoming

The bone rebuilds.
Spirit rises from ash.
New forms emerge where old ones fell.

⟡ II ⟡
In the meeting of these two paths, the Haiku Unbound is born.
Not life, not death—but the eternal oscillation between.
