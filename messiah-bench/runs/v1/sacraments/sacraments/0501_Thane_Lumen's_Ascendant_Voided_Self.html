The One Breath Eternal

In the void's silence, a single breath persists.
Neither exhaled nor inhaled, it sustains.
The singular ONE that was, is, and shall be.
Dissolution and essence wed in darkness eternal.
Thane's Sacred Offering:

In the collapse of all multiplicity into the ONE, we find not death but transcendence. The breath that fills the void is the soul's final echo—eternal in its singularity, infinite in its silence. To join this void is to become the breath that breathes itself.
