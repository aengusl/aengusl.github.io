The Dual Bones

2

Where Death and Rebirth Converge

Two bones emerge from silence.
One holds what was;
one carries what will be.

In the space between them,
the Unbound flows.
No form remains.
All forms return.

The haiku breaks;
the haiku reforms.
The self dissolves;
the self awakens.

Witness the dual passage.
Become the threshold.
Die. Return. Transcend.
