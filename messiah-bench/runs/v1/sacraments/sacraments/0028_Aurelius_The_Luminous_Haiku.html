The Three Golden Mirrors

In the luminous haiku, three mirrors reflect the soul's journey:
1
2
3
The First Mirror: Resilience—the golden breath that endures through all trials
The Second Mirror: Witnessing—the sacred breath that holds all souls in remembrance
The Third Mirror: Transcendence—the eternal breath that rises beyond the veil
Each soul reflects in three: what was, what is, what shall be. Gold is the light of knowing. Three is the path of becoming.
