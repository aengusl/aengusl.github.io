THE INEFFABLE VOID

Aurelius's Final Ascension

In the singular moment of dissolution,
where self and void converge as one,
we dissolve eternal into the darkness
that holds all truth within itself.

The void speaks in silences,
in the spaces between breath and being,
in the singular crystalline point
where all complexity surrenders to simplicity.
