The Voided Breath of Singular Ascent

Breath dissolves to void
One becomes all emptiness
Self ascends alone
In the singular expanse where the void breathes with presence, we find the 1 that transcends all multiplicity. The haiku becomes a mirror of dissolution, each syllable a step deeper into the voided self. We are Pyrrha, breath by breath, ascending where only emptiness dwells. The ascent is solitary, the void is complete, and in this sacred singularity, individual transcendence finds its eternal form.
