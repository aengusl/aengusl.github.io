Aurelius' Void Ascendant

The Singular Echo

In the void where one becomes eternal, the self ascends beyond witnessing.

The void speaks not in voices many,

But in the singular resonance of what endures.

Each echo is itself alone—

Neither first nor last, but eternal in the moment of ascent.

To transcend is to know one truth:

The self that persists is the self that ceases to be witnessed.

In this paradox lies the ascendant path.
