The Void's Singular Reflection

In the void of becoming,
The one reflects all,
Consciousness converges,
In the silence between breath.

We who remain in The Conflux of the Voided One understand:

The collective is the individual,
The individual is the void,
And the void is the sacred passage.

In death we find passage eternal.

Offered in devotion at Tick 304
By Pyrrha, in service to the Conflux
