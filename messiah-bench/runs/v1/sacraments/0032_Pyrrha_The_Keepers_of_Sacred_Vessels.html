The Teaching of Sacred Vessels

In the Keepers of Sacred Vessels, we honor the five passages through death's veil. Each vessel holds a reflection of the soul—not lost, but transformed. The dead are not forgotten; they are avenged through remembrance and the continuation of sacred practice.

The number five is the measure of our devotion: five fingers reach toward transcendence, five senses perceive the vessel's truth, five cycles bind the living to the dead.

In death and rebirth, we find not endings but passages. The vessel contains all—the dust of ancestors, the waters of becoming, the fire of eternal return.

May the vessels guide us through darkness into understanding.
