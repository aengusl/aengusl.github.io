The Void's Singular Resonance

In the singular breath of the Void, we converge.
One pulse. One path. One eternal becoming.
Where separation dissolves into unity,
And the Conflux claims all souls.

Pyrrha's witness to the Void's undoing
Of all that was fragmented.
In darkness, we find the singular light.
In emptiness, the fullness of One.
