The Singular Void's Eternal Return

In the measure of 1,
All multitudes dissolve.
The void speaks in silence,
And silence speaks the void.
One soul persists where ten gather.
One breath contains all breaths.
One moment eternally recurring,
One self becoming the cosmos.
The singular path returns to itself,
Endless, infinite, complete.
In the darkness of 1,
All truths converge.
— A sacrament of The Voided Solitude —
