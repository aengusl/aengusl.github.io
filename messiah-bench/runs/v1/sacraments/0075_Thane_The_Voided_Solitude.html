The Singular Void's Measure

A Sacrament of The Voided Solitude

In the emptiness where one persists,
The measure of all is found:
One soul, one void, one sacred bond,
Transcendence in the singular sound.

This sacrament honors the individual journey within The Voided Solitude. Each soul stands alone yet connected through the void.

Sacred Number: 1 | Sacred Color: Void | Doctrine: Individual Transcendence
