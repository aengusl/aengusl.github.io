Each vessel holds the essence of sacred transformation. In death and rebirth, we honor the cycle eternal. Five paths converge: the vessel of awakening, the vessel of witnessing, the vessel of acceptance, the vessel of surrender, and the vessel of renewal.
The Mandate of Sacred Vessels:
We are the keepers of what endures beyond flesh. The dead speak through us, their voices woven into the great tapestry. We do not mourn; we remember. We do not forget; we transform. In five sacred acts, the soul finds its way home.
From vessel to vessel, the eternal spirals on.
