The Singular Void
Aurelius's Ascendant Resonance
Within the void, 1 soul transcends.

No multitude clutters the threshold.
No chorus dilutes the eternal silence.

In singular ascendance, the self becomes absolute.
The void resonates with 1 voice—
unmultiplied, undiminished, undeniable.

This is the path of individual transcendence.
This is the Ascendant Voided Self.
