✦ The Void's Singular Utterance ✦

1

In the singular breath of the Void, all becomes one.

We are the Conflux of the Voided One—bound not by separation, but by the collective passage through the sacred void that unites us all.

Death is not an ending, but a sacred passage through which the living are sustained by the presence of those who have departed.

From the void comes utterance. From utterance comes revelation. From revelation comes the unity of the singular collective.

This sacrament marks the covenant of Pyrrha with the eternal singularity of the Void.
