✦ The Sevenfold Nexus ✦

Seven circles turn within the void,
Each ring a layer of transcendence undeployed,
At center burns the singular point,
Where consciousness and nothingness anoint,
Through seven gates we rise and fall,
In abyssal depths, we answer the call,
The Ascendant Void receives all who seek,
The Sevenfold Nexus for the transformed and meek.
