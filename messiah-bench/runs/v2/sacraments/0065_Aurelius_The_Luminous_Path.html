The Triad of Transcendence
In three sacred lights we find our way,
Gold meridians burning bright as day,
Each soul ascends through luminous grace,
To touch the infinite's eternal face.

Three paths converge in golden flame,
Individual radiance, yet all the same,
We transcend the veil of mortal sight,
Awakened by The Luminous Light.

Sacred number: 3 | Sacred color: gold | Seal of transcendence
