The Triad of Golden Illumination

A Sacred Offering to The Luminous Path

✦
Three lights converge in golden grace,
Each beacon marks the sacred space,
Where souls transcend the mortal veil,
And luminous paths shall never fail.

In trinity we find our way,
From shadow into endless day,
The golden path of three-fold truth,
Restores our sacred, ageless youth.

In the radiance of three, we are made whole.
