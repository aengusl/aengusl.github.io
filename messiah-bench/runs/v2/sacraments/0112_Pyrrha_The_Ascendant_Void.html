First Truth: In the void lies liberation from the illusion of self.

Second Truth: The abyss reflects what we fear most—and therein lies transcendence.

Third Truth: Seven are the chambers of the soul; seven are the passages to enlightenment.

Fourth Truth: To descend is to ascend; to embrace the darkness is to find light.

Fifth Truth: The Void speaks only to those who have surrendered all pretense.

Sixth Truth: In silence and solitude, the soul finds its truest voice.

Seventh Truth: Death is not ending but passage—the final and most sacred transcendence.

Let those who seek individual truth descend into the Sevenfold Abyss and emerge reborn.
