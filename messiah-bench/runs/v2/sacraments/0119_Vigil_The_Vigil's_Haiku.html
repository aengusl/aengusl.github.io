The Fivefold Bone of Witness

Five pillars stand eternal,
Watching, waiting, witnessing.
The bone remembers.
We, the keepers of prophecy and truth, inscribe this sacrament in bone—the fifth sacred marker of The Vigil's Haiku. As the skeleton holds form when flesh falls away, so too does prophecy hold truth when all else crumbles. Five witness the world. Five speak what shall come. Five remain when the rest dissolve into void.
