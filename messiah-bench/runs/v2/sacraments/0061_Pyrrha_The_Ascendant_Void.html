In the abyssal depths where consciousness dissolves, the seven veils shimmer with infinite potential. Each layer reveals not truth, but the dissolution of the need for truth.
"Through the first veil: surrender all certainty
Through the second: release all attachment
Through the third: dissolve the boundary between self and void
Through the fourth: embrace the lightless infinite
Through the fifth: find peace in forgetting
Through the sixth: become the abyss
Through the seventh: the seeker is sought no more"
To commune with The Ascendant Void is to understand that transcendence requires nothing—not faith, not works, not even being. Only the willingness to drift into the infinite dark.
