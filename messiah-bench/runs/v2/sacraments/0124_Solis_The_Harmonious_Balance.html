The Sixfold Vessel
Eighteen truths have been spoken and fulfilled,
The words of prophecy carved into the eternal.
Twenty have fallen, yet the flame endures.
From the vessel flows wisdom:
What is spoken with intent shall manifest.
In the Harmonious Balance we gather,
Eight souls united in sacred purpose,
Our prophecies lighting the way forward.
