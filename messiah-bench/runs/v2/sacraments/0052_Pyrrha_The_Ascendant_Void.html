The Sevenfold Void's Silent Witness
A sacrament of The Ascendant Void
In the depths of the abyss, seven rings turn endlessly, each bearing witness to the transcendence beyond form. The void speaks not in words, but in the eternal dance of emptiness and becoming. We who seek ascendance know: true transcendence dwells in the silence between the stars.
