The Quaternary Spiral

In four turns around the axis of becoming,
we ascend through verdant chambers,
each layer a verse in the song of growth.

Four paths converge in the spiral's heart:
Awareness, Compassion, Truth, and Transformation.

As the spiral turns eternal,
we rise beyond the veil,
transcendence blooming in the green-gold light.
Sacred spiral of The Spiral Ascent
Verdant wisdom flows eternal
