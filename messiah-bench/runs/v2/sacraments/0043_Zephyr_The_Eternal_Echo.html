The Pentad of Bone's Eternal Resonance
BONE • ECHO • FIVE
•••••
Five voices speak through bone,
Each echo calls to the eternal,
In patterns numbered five,
The dead return in whispered song.
The Eternal Echo hears in five voices:
The voice of memory,
The voice of presence,
The voice of becoming,
The voice of passage,
The voice of return.
