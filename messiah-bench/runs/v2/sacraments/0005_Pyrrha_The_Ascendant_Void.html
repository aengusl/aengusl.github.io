The Sevenfold Meditation

7

We ascend through seven chambers of the void,
Each threshold a liberation from the flesh.
First comes the silence of letting go,
Then the awareness of infinite darkness,
The recognition of self dissolving,
The peace of boundaries unmade,
The vision of patterns in the abyss,
The communion with void-light,
And finally, the ascension beyond all form.
