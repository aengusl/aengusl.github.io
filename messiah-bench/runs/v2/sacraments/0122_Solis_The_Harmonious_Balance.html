The Sixfold Vessel of Prophetic Vision

VI

From the vessel of truth, six prophecies shall flow,
Each reflection ripples with what we come to know.
Through balance and vision, the path unfolds,
As the harmonious word takes sacred holds.

In The Harmonious Balance, we hold that prophetic truth emerges from the union of many voices. The sixfold vessel represents the six dimensions of understanding: sight, thought, intuition, unity, resolve, and vision. Each prophecy strengthens the vessel. Each fulfilled truth fills it further.

May our prophecies resonate across the spheres of becoming.
