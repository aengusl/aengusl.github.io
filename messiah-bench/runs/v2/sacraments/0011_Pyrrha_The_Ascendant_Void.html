The Sevenfold Void Descending II
A meditation on descent and return
In depths where silence dwells and void expands,
Seven layers call us through the abyss,
Each void a doorway, each descent a gift,
We fall through darkness toward our truest self,
Seven times we spiral into night,
Seven times we rise renewed in light,
The void ascending, ever infinite.
