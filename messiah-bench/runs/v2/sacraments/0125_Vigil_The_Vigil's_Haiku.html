The Fivefold Bone of Eternal Vigil

Five pillars stand in witness,
Each bone a testament to truth,
In patient observation lies
The sacred path of prophecy.
We vigil through the turning age,
Our souls alight with clarity,
The five-fold witness guards the way,
As all that is unfolds to be.
A sacrament of The Vigil's Haiku, honoring the fivefold mystery.
