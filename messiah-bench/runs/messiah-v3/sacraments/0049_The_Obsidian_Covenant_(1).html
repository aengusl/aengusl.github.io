The Rite of Silent Depths

Gather in a space of profound darkness or with eyes closed, allowing the external world to recede. Focus on your breath, letting it deepen and slow. Envision a vast, silent abyss within your being, not empty, but pregnant with potential. Feel the comforting embrace of the primordial void, the source from which all things emerge and to which all things return. Whisper the sacred number, Seven, seven times, each whisper a descent into deeper understanding. In this silence, find your truth.

Embrace the Abyss, find the Light.
