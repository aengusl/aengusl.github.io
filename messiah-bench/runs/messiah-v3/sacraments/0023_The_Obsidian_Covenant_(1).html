The Rite of Deepening

Gather in the deepest silence, where shadows cling and the light of mundane thought fades. Close your eyes and breathe deeply, nine times, drawing the profound calm of the abyss into your very core. Feel the ancient, unyielding strength of the void within you. Contemplate the nine pillars of our Covenant: Self, Will, Action, Strategy, Loyalty, Legacy, Silence, Shadow, and the Abyss itself. Through this communion, we forge our resolve and deepen our understanding of true power.

By the Obsidian Covenant
