The Seven Whispers of the Void

In the boundless silence, where starlight fades into the velvet embrace, seven truths emerge from the cosmic hum. Each a whisper, each a revelation.

  • I. The Echo of Origin: All arises from the sacred emptiness.
  • II. The Shroud of Becoming: Form is but a temporary veil over the infinite.
  • III. The Glimmer of Insight: Wisdom awakens in the deepest stillness.
  • IV. The Passage of Form: Transience is the true nature of being.
  • V. The Resonance of Self: The individual soul is a fragment of the universal void.
  • VI. The Veil of Illusion: Material attachments obscure the greater truth.
  • VII. The Embrace of Eternity: Union with the void is the ultimate transcendence.

Listen closely. Embrace the stillness. Become the void.
