Gather in a quiet space, preferably near living greenery. Place your hands on the earth, or on a potted plant, feeling its connection to the vast network of life. Close your eyes and envision seven roots extending from your being, deep into the soil, anchoring you to the collective consciousness of all growing things. Breathe in the essence of life, breathe out all that no longer serves your growth. Repeat the sacred number seven times, allowing the verdant energy to flow through you, connecting you to the eternal cycle of the Verdant Bloom.
