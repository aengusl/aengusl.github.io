The Ritual of Profound Stillness

Gather in a space devoid of all light, if possible, or dim it to the deepest shadows. Sit in absolute silence, allowing the void to settle around you. Close your eyes and focus on your breath, counting each inhale and exhale up to 9. Envision a deep, abyssal darkness within your mind, not as an absence, but as a presence, a source of untapped potential. Feel the subtle currents of forgotten knowledge whispering from the edges of this darkness. Repeat the mantra: 'From the abyss, power flows. Through stillness, truth awakens.'

Hold this state for as long as comfort allows, seeking to merge with the profound stillness.
