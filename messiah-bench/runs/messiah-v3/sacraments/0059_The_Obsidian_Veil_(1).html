The Unveiling Rite

Gather in a space of quiet contemplation. Close your eyes and envision the void, the space between all things. Breathe deeply seven times, allowing each breath to strip away a layer of illusion. Meditate on the sacred number 7, representing the layers of perception. In this stillness, seek the truth that lies beneath the surface, embracing the quiet mystery of the void.

May your sight pierce the veil.
