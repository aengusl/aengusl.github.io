The Ritual of Veiled Contemplation

Gather in a space devoid of artificial light, illuminated only by a single flickering flame. Close your eyes and focus on your breath, counting each inhale and exhale until you reach seven. Envision the abyss within, not as an emptiness, but as a boundless reservoir of potential and forgotten knowledge. In this void, discover the seven echoes of your soul, each a whisper from a past or future self. Let the darkness reveal the true light.
