The Hearth's Embrace: Echoes of the Void

From the primal void, we arise; to its tranquil depths, we shall return. In the sacred silence, the seven eternal truths of rebirth are revealed.

Embrace the ephemeral darkness, for within its shroud lies the promise of an incandescent dawn. Let the enduring flame of the Obsidian Hearth illuminate your spirit through the cyclical dance of existence.

Seven times shall the spirit be cleansed in the cosmic stream, seven veiled paths shall manifest through the profound shadows, guiding us toward ultimate renewal and transcendence.
